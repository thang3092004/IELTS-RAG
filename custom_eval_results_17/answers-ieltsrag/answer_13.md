### Integration of Intangible Attributes  
One of the most significant advancements brought by Decision Lens is its ability to incorporate **intangible attributes** into player evaluations. Decision Lens provides a structured framework that allows teams to systematically assess these qualities alongside traditional performance metrics. By doing so, teams can create a more comprehensive profile of each player, leading to better-informed decisions during the selection process.

The methodology employed by Decision Lens is designed to prioritize player selection based on a combination of **quantifiable data** and **qualitative insights**. The automation of these processes not only saves time but also enhances the accuracy of evaluations, reducing the likelihood of human error in decision-making.
