The affordability aspect also encourages broader adoption and experimentation with RAG systems. As these technologies become more accessible, businesses and researchers are likely to explore diverse applications that can benefit from improved context handling, ultimately driving innovation.

### 2. Retrieval Accuracy 

Gemini Flash's capabilities in handling long contexts contribute to improving retrieval accuracy.
