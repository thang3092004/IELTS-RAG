Vision-based RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) can offer advantages over text-based RAG by providing richer contextual information through visual data, which can enhance understanding and interpretation. It allows for the integration of visual cues and context that are not present in text alone, potentially leading to more accurate and contextually relevant responses. Additionally, vision-based RAG can be particularly beneficial in scenarios where visual information is crucial, such as in tasks involving image recognition or interpretation.
