Anthropic's prompt caching mechanism is designed to optimize the processing of repeated prompts by storing and reusing the results of previous computations. This reduces the need to recompute responses for identical or similar prompts, thereby saving computational resources and improving response times. On the other hand, Gemini context caching focuses on maintaining context across interactions by storing relevant information from previous exchanges. This allows for more coherent and contextually aware responses in ongoing conversations. The key difference lies in the focus: prompt caching is about efficiency in handling repeated prompts, while context caching is about maintaining continuity and coherence in interactions.
