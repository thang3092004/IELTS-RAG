LightRAG is a simpler and more cost-efficient version of GraphRAG. It achieves similar performance to GraphRAG but at a much lower cost. LightRAG uses a smaller, more affordable model like GPT-401 mini compared to GraphRAG which uses the full GPT-4 model. This makes LightRAG more accessible and suitable for small and medium-sized businesses. However, LightRAG still has some limitations compared to GraphRAG. It cannot handle extremely long input documents and contexts, and its performance degrades for very long contexts. But for most practical applications, LightRAG provides an effective and cost-efficient alternative to GraphRAG.
