## Advanced RAG Techniques and Their Solutions to Basic RAG Limitations

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) is an innovative approach that integrates retrieval systems with generative techniques to enhance the performance of AI models, particularly in the realm of information retrieval. While basic RAG techniques have paved the way for more accurate data retrieval and response generation, several advanced methodologies have emerged to address inherent limitations associated with standard RAG systems.
