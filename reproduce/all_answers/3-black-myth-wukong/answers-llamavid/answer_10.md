The 'Self Advance' system enhances the player's combat abilities by allowing them to use relics that grant them temporary boosts, such as increased attack power, health regeneration, and invisibility. This allows the player to take on stronger enemies and bosses, and provides them with a competitive advantage. The system is designed to make the player feel like a hero, with abilities similar to those of Sun Wukong and other legendary monkey warriors. The relics are obtained by defeating enemies and bosses, and can be upgraded to provide even greater boosts. The system is an example of how the game uses fantasy elements to give the player an epic adventure and sense of accomplishment.
