The statues of the Jade Emperor serve as a way to represent the Jade Emperor, a deity in Chinese mythology, in the game's world. The Jade Emperor is the ruler of the heavens and the realm of the Jade Emperor, which is where the game takes place. The statues are used to indicate that the area or location is associated with the Jade Emperor and his court. They are also used as markers to help the player navigate and identify locations, such as when they are exploring the land of Thunderclap. The Jade Emperor is an important figure in Chinese mythology, so the statues serve to convey the game's Chinese theme and influence.
