In Chapter 5, the group encounters the Tiger-Snake enemies who are led by Yellow Brow. The Tiger-Snake enemies have several strengths and weaknesses:

Strengths: 
- They have a variety of different attack styles, making them difficult to fight. 
- They are very aggressive and quick to attack. 
- They can use powerful spells like freezing spells to slow the group down.

Weaknesses:
- They are not as powerful as the Bull King's forces. 
- They lack the Bull King's fan which can be used as a weapon.
- They are vulnerable to being frozen by spells like the freezing gourd.

So in summary, the Tiger-Snake enemies are aggressive and quick, but lack the power and fan weapon of the Bull King's forces. Their freezing spells can be a weakness that can be exploited.
