The Knot of Voidness is a magical item that when activated allows the player to temporarily increase their attack and defense by 25%. It is an extremely useful item that can be used during battle to gain an advantage over enemies. The increased attack and defense allows the player to deal more damage and take more hits, making them a more formidable opponent. It is especially useful for fighting against tough enemies and bosses. The Knot of Voidness provides a significant boost in power and makes the player more formidable in combat.
