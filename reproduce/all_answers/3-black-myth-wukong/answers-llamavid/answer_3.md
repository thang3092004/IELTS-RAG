The game uses visual storytelling through its narrative and artwork to convey its themes of perseverance, wisdom, and self-discovery. The characters face numerous challenges and obstacles in their journey, representing the difficulties that come with the path of enlightenment. The artwork is also filled with symbolism and imagery that conveys deeper meanings, such as the power of persistence and the importance of wisdom. Through these visual storytelling elements, the game aims to engage players emotionally and intellectually, leaving a lasting impression of the journey and its themes.
