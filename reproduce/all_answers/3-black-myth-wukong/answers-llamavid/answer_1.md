The central conflicts in this story revolve around Sun Wukong and his journey to retrieve the relics that will allow him to achieve Buddhahood. The main conflicts include:

1. The conflict with Yellowbrow, who stole the seed sack containing the relics and fled to the Land of Thunderclap. Wukong and his disciples must confront Yellowbrow and recover the relics.

2. The conflict with the Yaksha Kings, who guard the relics and seek to prevent Wukong from obtaining them. Wukong and his disciples must defeat the Yaksha Kings to retrieve the relics.

3. The conflict with the Celestial Court, who seek to prevent Wukong from achieving Buddhahood. Wukong and his disciples must defeat the Celestial Court to attain Buddhahood.

The central resolutions involve:

1. Wukong and his disciples defeating Yellowbrow and recovering the relics.

2. Wukong and his disciples defeating the Yaksha Kings and obtaining the relics.

3. Wukong and his disciples defeating the Celestial Court and achieving Buddhahood.
