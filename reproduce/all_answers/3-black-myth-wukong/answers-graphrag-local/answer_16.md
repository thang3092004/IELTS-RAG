The behavior of the camera during combat with certain enemies in fantasy action games like "Nioh 2" and "Dark Souls III" serves to enhance engagement and the overall immersive experience. The interplay between dynamic camera angles, camera shifts, and adjustments aids players in effectively navigating the challenges posed by formidable foes. Here’s a breakdown of how the camera specifically behaves in these combat scenarios:

### Dynamic Camera Angles

Dynamic camera angles play a critical role in combat, providing varied perspectives that emphasize the action and intensity of each encounter. During battle sequences, the camera tends to shift angles that closely follow the actions of the player character and the opposing enemy, allowing for a more cinematic and immersive experience. These shifts can highlight key attacks, evasions, and the character's movement within the environmental context, allowing players to appreciate the intricacies of the combat.

### Engagement Enhancement

The strategic use of camera angles enhances player immersion during combat. By capturing exciting moments and showcasing dramatic lighting changes, such as flashes or colored highlights from powerful strikes, the camera contributes to the emotional weight of the conflicts faced by players. This visual feedback supports the gameplay by conveying the importance of tactical decisions and making combat feel more exhilarating.

### Reactions to Enemy Actions

The camera may also adapt based on specific enemy behaviors and attack patterns. It can zoom in or alter the perspective to provide a clear view of special enemy attacks or vulnerabilities. For example, when confronting larger enemies or bosses, the camera may position itself to give an overview of the battlefield, helping players to strategize their movements while maintaining awareness of their surroundings. 

### Environmental Interaction

When combined with the rocky or rugged terrains that players often navigate, the camera dynamics can help enhance the player’s overall combat strategy regarding positioning and movement. The camera adjusts not only to enhance the fearsome nature of certain foes but also responds to environmental hindrances, which may impact the player’s freedom of movement and tactics during engagements.

### Conclusion

In essence, the camera’s behavior during combat is designed to support player engagement through dynamic perspectives, facilitating a richer gameplay experience. By focusing on both the player character and the diverse enemy animations, the camera plays a pivotal role in shaping the intensity of combat encounters and allowing players to navigate intricate confrontations effectively. This tactical visual support reinforces both the narrative and action aspects of the gameplay, making every encounter feel unique and significant.
