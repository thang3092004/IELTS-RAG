The player character acquires new spells by learning them from other characters they encounter along their journey. In Part 1, after the player character defeats a mini-boss, they are taught the **Immobilize** spell by a character that resembles a monkey. This spell allows the player character to bind an enemy in place for a short time. Later in Part 2, the player character receives the **Disperse Illusions Traverse** spell from another monkey character after defeating a mini-boss that uses a similar spell. This spell allows the player character to escape by dispersing into mist.
