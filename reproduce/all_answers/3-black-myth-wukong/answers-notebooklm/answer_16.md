The sources describe an issue where the camera moves erratically during combat with certain enemies. The player mentions this happening in two different chapters and with two different enemy types.

*   In chapter 4, the player describes the camera "going all weird" when fighting centipede-like enemies. This makes it difficult to see and fight effectively, and they note that "the camera is really off".

*   In chapter 5, the player is frustrated when fighting firebats because "the camera goes all weird and shit". This happens again when fighting a group of insect enemies, causing them to complain about the camera.

The player does not know why the camera behaves this way. It may be a bug in the game or an intentional design choice to increase the challenge of fighting certain enemies.
