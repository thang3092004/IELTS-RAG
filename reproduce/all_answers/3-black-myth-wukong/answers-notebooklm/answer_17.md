The **Keeper Shrines**, also referred to as meditation posts, serve multiple purposes in the game world of *Black Myth Wukong*.

At Keeper Shrines players can:

*   **Fully restore health and replenish their gourd:** Offering incense at a Keeper Shrine fully restores the player's health and replenishes their healing gourd.
*   **Respawn enemies:** Resting at a shrine respawns all defeated enemies in the area.
*   **Fast travel:** Players can instantly travel to any Keeper Shrine where incense has already been offered.
*   **Cultivate spirits:** The spirits of defeated *yagu* can be cultivated at Keeper Shrines to enhance their Spirit Skill.
*   **Reallocate attribute points:** Players can completely reconfigure the attributes they have spent their Spark points on.
*   **Buy and sell items:** The shops found at Keeper Shrines allow players to buy and sell items.
*   **Craft items:** Players can craft items, such as pills and weapons, at Keeper Shrines.
*   **Retrieve lost spirits:** Players can retrieve spirits from defeated *yagu* that they did not absorb during combat.

The sources do not provide an explanation for the lore or significance behind the Keeper Shrines in the game world.
