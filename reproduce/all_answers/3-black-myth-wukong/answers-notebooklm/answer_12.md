The sources describe three staff stances or forms associated with the staff in the video game *Black Myth: Wukong*, each affecting the player's combat approach in a different way:

*   **Pillar Stance:** In source, the player unlocks the Pillar stance, which increases critical hit chance. This suggests that this stance is best for players who want to inflict a high amount of damage.
*   **Thrust Stance:** This stance focuses on dealing damage with quick thrusting attacks. Source mentions that the player decides to focus on Thrust stance in Chapter 4 due to acquiring the Spike Shaft staff, a weapon that boosts Thrust stance damage. This stance appears to be effective for players who prioritize quick and precise strikes.
*   **Whirlwind Stance:** This stance, as mentioned in source, is associated with the Wind Bear staff. Its fourth light attack and mobile staff spin can "stir up a whirlwind of sand". This implies an emphasis on area-of-effect attacks and crowd control.

Each stance allows the player to adapt their combat style to different situations and enemy types.
