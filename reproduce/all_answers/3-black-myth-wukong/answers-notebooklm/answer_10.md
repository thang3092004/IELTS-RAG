The "Self Advance" system in Black Myth: Wukong allows players to awaken talents and level up to gain Sparks, which unlock and enhance abilities and spells. Sparks are not lost upon death. Players can allocate these Sparks into three main categories: **Vigor, Stamina, and Proficiency**. 

Here's how these categories enhance combat abilities:

*   **Vigor increases maximum health**, making the player more durable.  One example is strengthening flexibility and staff stances, which allow for new stances and moderately increases maximum health per talent level.
*   **Stamina improves actions related to movement and dodging.** Each talent level moderately reduces the stamina cost of sprinting and dodging. This allows for more agile maneuvers in combat.
*   **Proficiency enhances combat techniques and spell effectiveness.** For example, investing in proficiency increases the engagement distance of light attacks or extends the duration of immobilization spells.

Additionally, **players can reallocate Sparks at any time**, allowing for flexibility and customization based on combat situations and playstyle preferences.
