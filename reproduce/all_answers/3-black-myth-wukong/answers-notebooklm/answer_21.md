The "Knot of Voidness" relic, obtained at the end of Chapter 3 of *Black Myth: Wukong*, allows the player to **craft new armor and weapons.** The Knot of Voidness itself does not directly enhance any of the player's abilities. It is instead a crafting material used by the blacksmith character. The sources also indicate that the Knot of Voidness can be used to **craft Celestial medicines**.
