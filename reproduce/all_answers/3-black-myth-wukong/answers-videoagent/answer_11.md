The spear has distinct combat advantages over the staff due to its pointed tip, which allows for thrusting attacks that can penetrate armor or reach vital organs. The spear's length provides a reach advantage, keeping opponents at a distance. Additionally, the spear can be used for both thrusting and slashing motions, offering versatility in combat. In contrast, a staff is primarily a blunt weapon used for striking and blocking, lacking the piercing capability of a spear.
