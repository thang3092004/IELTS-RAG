Linking feedback to specific parts of writing can be challenging without Canvas because it requires a manual process of identifying and referencing specific sections of the text. Without a platform like Canvas, which allows for inline comments and annotations, feedback may become less precise and harder to associate with the exact part of the writing it pertains to. This can lead to confusion for the writer, as they may struggle to understand which parts of their work need improvement. Additionally, without a centralized system, managing and organizing feedback can become cumbersome for both the reviewer and the writer.
