ChatGPT Search offers several advantages as a default search engine, according to OpenAI product lead, Kevin Wheel. 

**It combines real-time information access with ChatGPT's conversational capabilities.** Users don't have to switch between a search engine and ChatGPT; they can ask follow-up questions and refine their search within a single interface.

**It understands conversational language and search context.**  Users can naturally interact with it like in a conversation and don’t need to rephrase searches with specific keywords. ChatGPT Search understands the context from previous questions.

**It provides faster navigation to known websites.** For users who already know their destination, ChatGPT Search provides quick links at the top of the results, making navigation more efficient. 

**It offers rich visual results directly within ChatGPT.** Users can see images, videos, and even maps related to their search without having to leave the ChatGPT interface. These visual elements enhance the search experience and make it more engaging.

**It leverages ChatGPT's intelligence for better search understanding.** ChatGPT Search combines traditional web search results with ChatGPT's ability to comprehend complex language and user intent. This results in a search experience that is tailored to the user’s needs and provides more relevant results.

**It’s available on all platforms where you use ChatGPT.** Users can use it on the web, iPhone, and Android apps, providing a consistent search experience across different devices.
