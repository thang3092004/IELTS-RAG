*   **Real-time Visual Content Sharing**: Advanced Voice Mode allows users to share their phone camera or screen in real-time to receive richer feedback and make conversations more helpful and interactive.
*   **Increased Accessibility**: Voice interactions can be more accessible for users who may find typing difficult or prefer speaking to typing. 
*   **Hands-Free Interaction**: Advanced Voice Mode can be useful in situations where typing is not feasible, such as while driving or cooking.
