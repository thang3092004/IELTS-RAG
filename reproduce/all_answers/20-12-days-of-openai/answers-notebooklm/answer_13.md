Using an image to initiate video generation in Sora is a simple process. First, navigate to the **storyboard** section of the Sora platform.  At the top of the screen, you will find a series of storyboard cards. **Upload the image you wish to use as the starting point for your video into the first storyboard card.** Sora will then automatically populate the first card with your image and create a second card with a continuation caption. This caption provides a suggested direction for the video based on Sora's analysis of your image. You can edit this caption, adjust the position of the cards on the timeline, or leave it as is.  **Once you are satisfied, simply click "create" and Sora will generate a video based on your image and the provided caption.** You can then further refine your creation using other tools, such as adding more storyboard cards for additional actions or utilizing features like loop and blend.
