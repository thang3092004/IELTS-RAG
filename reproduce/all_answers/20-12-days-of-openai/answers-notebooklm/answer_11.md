The 'Storyboard' feature allows users to create videos with multiple actions using a timeline. Here is how to use the Storyboard feature:

*   **Describe the scene:** In the first storyboard card, describe the environment, characters, and initial action. The level of detail provided will determine how much Sora fills in. For instance, you could input "a beautiful white crane stands in a creek."
*   **Sequence actions:** The timeline displays an outline of the entire clip. By clicking anywhere within this outline, users can add additional cards to sequence the video's actions. For example, you can add a card that says "the crane dips its head into the water and pulls out a fish."
*   **Adjust timing:** The space between cards on the timeline represents the time Sora will use to connect the actions. This can be adjusted to control the pacing and create cinematic cuts or continuous shots.
*   **Use images:** Users can upload images to create video. Sora will automatically analyze the image and add a continuation caption, understanding the potential movement and filling in the details. Users can edit this caption or adjust the image's position on the timeline.
*   **Generate the video:** After setting up the storyboard cards and adjusting the timeline, users can hit 'create' to generate the video. Sora will then render the video based on the provided instructions and images.

The Storyboard feature offers a user-friendly and intuitive way to direct multi-action videos, giving users more control over the creative process.
