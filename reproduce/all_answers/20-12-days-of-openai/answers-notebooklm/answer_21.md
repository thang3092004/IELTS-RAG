The sources do not mention how Thompson Reuters utilized reinforcement fine-tuning to enhance their Co-Counsel AI tool.  They do discuss how **OpenAI used reinforcement fine-tuning to help train their own frontier models like GPT-4 and the O-1 series.** Reinforcement fine-tuning helps models learn to reason in new ways over custom domains. This technique was used to help train a model to predict which gene might be responsible for a genetic disease given a list of symptoms. The process involves giving the model space to think through the problem, then grading the final answer. Through reinforcement learning, lines of thinking that led to correct answers are reinforced and lines of thinking that led to incorrect answers are disincentivized.
