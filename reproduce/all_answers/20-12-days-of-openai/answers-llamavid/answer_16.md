First, it allows users to directly search the web within the chat window, enabling them to quickly find relevant information for their question. Second, the search results are presented as rich visual images and lists, making it much easier for users to understand and engage with the content. This visual representation of the search results is a significant improvement over traditional search results. Third, the search results are integrated directly within the chat, allowing users to interact with the content in the same way they interact with the rest of ChatGPT. This makes it much easier for users to get the information they need and apply it to their question. Overall, the integration of search in the Advanced Voice Mode provides a much more powerful and efficient way for users to find and engage with relevant information.
