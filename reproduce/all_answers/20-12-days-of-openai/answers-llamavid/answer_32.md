In Sora, 'blend' is a creative tool that allows users to describe changes to their video prompts and see how the model will generate a new output. By blending two or more different video elements, Sora can create a seamless and cohesive visual experience. For example, a user could describe a scene with a fish swimming in the water, a crane moving over the water, and a woolly mammoth walking across the desert. Sora would then generate a new video that combines these elements in a new way, such as having the fish swim over the crane and the woolly mammoth walking through the water. Blend allows users to create new and unique visual experiences by combining different elements in creative ways. This helps Sora extend beyond just generating video from text prompts, and enables it to generate more complex and interesting visuals.
