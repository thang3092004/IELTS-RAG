The introduction of 'storyboard' in Sora allows users to create a visual storyboard of their video content before generating the final video. This can be a very useful tool for video creators, especially for those who are not experienced with video editing software. With a storyboard, users can easily see the overall flow and structure of their content before generating the final video. They can see the different shots, scenes, and transitions in a visual form before committing to generating the actual video. This allows users to quickly identify any issues or problems with the storyboard before generating the final video. It can also help them make adjustments and changes to their storyboards before generating the final video. Overall, the storyboard tool in Sora is designed to make video creation and editing easier and more intuitive for users with less experience with video editing software.
