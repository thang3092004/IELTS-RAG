The concept of 'presets' in Sora enhances the creative process by allowing users to create a collection of pre-built storyboards that can be easily customized and modified. This provides a starting point for the user's video generation workflow, saving time and effort in setting up the initial components. Presets also allow users to build on existing generations and add new ideas, which helps develop creative flow and storytelling. With presets, users can quickly and easily generate a new generation of video content from the same prompt, while maintaining the core concepts and visual elements of the previous generation.
