The 'Explore' feature in Sora is a tool that allows users to generate new video content from text prompts, images, and other media. It can create videos from scratch or remix existing video content. This feature is designed to push the boundaries of what's possible in visual generation. The goal of Explore is to empower storytelling and provide a creative workflow environment where users can view their generations and look at bookmarks from the feed. The Explore feature also includes a simple composer with text or uploading an image, a timeline to set the order of cards, and a remix feature to create new clips from existing video. The Explore tool is designed to allow users to generate a wide variety of video content from a simple text prompt or image, and it provides a powerful set of creative tools to help them generate their own video content.
