Saint-Gobain has implemented several strategies to engage and align its diverse workforce of 168,000 employees around sustainability goals.  The company believes that **clearly aligning its vision, purpose, and strategic plan around sustainability has been instrumental in uniting employees towards a common goal**.  Here are some specific approaches they have taken:

*   **Reorganized the company by country to provide comprehensive solutions**: Instead of focusing on individual product lines, Saint-Gobain restructured itself to empower country CEOs with a full range of building materials. This shift enables them to deliver complete solutions for facades, acoustic performance, and other sustainability needs, fostering a more holistic and impactful approach. 
*   **Empowered local leadership**: By appointing country CEOs who are native to their respective countries, Saint-Gobain ensures a strong understanding of local market needs and cultural nuances. This localized leadership fosters greater responsiveness and effectiveness in implementing sustainability initiatives. 
*   **Integrated sustainability into decision-making**: Saint-Gobain implemented an internal carbon price, requiring all capital expenditures, acquisitions, and R&D projects to factor in the environmental impact.  This policy ensures that sustainability is embedded in every investment decision, regardless of the country or project. 
*   **Fostered a culture of trust, empowerment, and collaboration (TEC)**: The company promotes trust in its managers, empowers them to make decisions, and encourages collaboration across countries and departments.  This TEC approach enables employees to take ownership of sustainability initiatives and contribute their unique expertise.  
*   **Invested in open innovation**: Saint-Gobain actively seeks partnerships with architects, installers, customers, homeowners, and other stakeholders who share their sustainability values.  This open innovation approach fosters the exchange of ideas and accelerates the development and adoption of sustainable solutions. 

**The company emphasizes that this alignment around sustainability is not limited to younger generations, but rather extends across all demographics within the organization**.  This widespread commitment, combined with the aforementioned strategies, has enabled Saint-Gobain to achieve significant progress in reducing its environmental footprint while continuing to grow its business.
