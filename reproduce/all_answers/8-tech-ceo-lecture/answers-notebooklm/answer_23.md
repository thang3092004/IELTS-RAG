Rajesh Jain believes a strong company culture requires two elements: **an icon and a storyteller**. The **icon** is someone within the company who lives and breathes the company's values, serving as a living example. The **storyteller** is someone who relates the company's history and achievements to new recruits.  Jain uses his company, Netcore, as an example, where the current CEO serves as the icon and the head of HR as the storyteller. He believes that building a culture in this way helps employees feel connected to the company beyond just their paycheck.
