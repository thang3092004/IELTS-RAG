## Saint-Gobain's Approach to Engaging and Aligning Workforce on Sustainability Goals

Saint-Gobain, a leader in sustainable construction solutions, emphasizes the importance of engaging its diverse workforce in aligning with sustainability goals. The company's approach includes fostering a culture of sustainability, enhancing education and involvement, and implementing specific strategic initiatives aimed at addressing environmental challenges.

### Fostering a Culture of Sustainability

Saint-Gobain places significant emphasis on creating a cultural foundation where sustainability is viewed as a core business value. This is reflected in their commitment to the principles of the Circular Economy, aiming to minimize waste and maximize resource efficiency throughout their operations. The company promotes a collaborative atmosphere where employees across various regions can contribute to sustainability initiatives, ensuring that all voices are heard and valued.

### Enhancing Education and Involvement

A key aspect of aligning the workforce around sustainability goals is the emphasis on education. Saint-Gobain actively invests in training programs and workshops to enhance employee understanding of sustainability practices and the importance of their contributions. This educational outreach includes career development programs, such as internships with SGNA Saint-Gobain Research North America, which connect academic rigor with practical applications in sustainability.

The leadership also plays a crucial role in this endeavor. CEO Benoît Bazin frequently discusses sustainability during public forums and presentations, reinforcing the message that every employee’s involvement is essential in achieving the organization’s long-term sustainability targets.

### Strategic Initiatives and Reporting

Saint-Gobain has established specific sustainability goals and metrics to guide employee engagement. This includes initiatives aimed at reducing CO2 emissions and enhancing energy efficiency within construction practices. The company has achieved significant milestones, such as a 27% reduction in CO2 emissions over five years, which reflects not only corporate commitment but also the collective effort of employees in driving these changes.

Moreover, the organization communicates progress and achievements related to sustainability goals to its workforce, promoting transparency and accountability. This active dialogue helps to motivate employees and align their personal values with the company’s environmental objectives.

### Recognizing Collective Effort

Ultimately, Saint-Gobain recognizes that achieving sustainability goals requires a concerted effort from its 168,000 employees worldwide. The company’s leadership emphasizes teamwork and shared objectives, promoting employee alignment with sustainability goals through ongoing discussions and recognition of contributions. By integrating sustainability into the daily operations and ethos of the company, Saint-Gobain fosters a unified front, driving innovation and environmental stewardship across all levels of its workforce. 

In summary, Saint-Gobain's approach to engaging and aligning its diverse workforce around sustainability involves creating a supportive culture, enhancing education, establishing strategic initiatives, and promoting accountability and recognition. This multifaceted strategy ensures that all employees are not only aware of sustainability goals but are also active participants in achieving them.
