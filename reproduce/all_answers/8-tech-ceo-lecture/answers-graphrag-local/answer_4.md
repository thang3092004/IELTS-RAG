### Essential Elements for Building a Strong Company Culture According to Rajesh Jain

Rajesh Jain emphasizes that two critical elements are necessary for establishing a robust company culture: **an icon and a storyteller.**

**1. The Icon:**
The "icon" refers to a person who embodies the values and principles of the company's culture. This individual acts as a living representation of the culture, demonstrating qualities like honesty, transparency, and a humane environment. By embodying these traits, the icon serves as a reference point for employees, reinforcing the culture and values of the organization. 

**2. The Storyteller:**
The "storyteller" is responsible for communicating the company’s stories and values to newcomers and the wider organization. This role is crucial in sharing narratives that illustrate what makes the company unique and valuable. The storyteller ensures that the essence of the company is conveyed effectively, helping to foster an understanding and appreciation of the culture among employees.

Together, these elements support a thriving workplace environment, where employees feel connected to the company's mission and ethos, ultimately contributing to a strong overall company culture.
