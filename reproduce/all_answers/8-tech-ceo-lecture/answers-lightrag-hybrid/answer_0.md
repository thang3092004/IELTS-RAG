Saint-Gobain, a multinational corporation specializing in sustainable construction and innovative materials, places a strong emphasis on research and development (R&D) as part of its strategic priorities. Here are the main focuses in R&D for the company:

### 1. **Sustainability Innovations**
Saint-Gobain is heavily invested in developing sustainable building materials and practices. The company targets innovations that minimize environmental impact, such as reducing CO2 emissions, promoting energy efficiency, and enhancing the use of recycled materials in construction. Their commitment to sustainability drives many of their R&D projects, reflecting a focus on achieving long-term environmental goals.

### 2. **Energy Efficiency**
Improving energy efficiency in buildings and construction processes is a key priority. Saint-Gobain actively seeks technology advancements that enable more efficient energy use in the built environment. This includes innovations in insulation materials and systems that reduce overall energy consumption in residential and commercial buildings.

### 3. **Collaboration with Educational Institutions**
The company highlights the importance of collaboration with universities and research centers worldwide to promote innovation. By engaging with academic institutions, Saint-Gobain aims to leverage cutting-edge research and attract new talent to drive innovative solutions in construction materials and processes.

### 4. **Light Construction Methods**
Saint-Gobain invests in research related to light construction techniques. These methods focus on utilizing lighter materials that not only lower material usage but also minimize associated CO2 emissions. The development of lightweight alternatives aligns with the company’s sustainability ethos and addresses modern construction challenges.

### 5. **Circular Economy Principles**
The company advocates for circular economy practices, which emphasize the continual use of resources through recycling and reusing materials. R&D efforts are directed towards enhancing resource efficiency and reducing waste, ensuring that the construction processes become more sustainable.

### 6. **Advanced Materials Science**
Research into advanced materials, such as low-carbon concrete and glass technologies, forms a critical component of their R&D initiatives. Saint-Gobain aims to innovate in materials science, creating products that have better performance characteristics while also addressing environmental concerns.

### **Conclusion**
In summary, Saint-Gobain’s R&D priorities focus on sustainability, energy efficiency, collaboration with academic partners, light construction techniques, circular economy principles, and advanced materials science. These areas reflect a strategic commitment to creating innovative and sustainable solutions that contribute to the future of the construction industry and environmental stewardship.
