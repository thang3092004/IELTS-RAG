## Main Research and Development Priorities for Saint-Gobain

Saint-Gobain emphasizes a strong commitment to sustainability, with its research and development (R&D) priorities reflecting this objective significantly. The company shall pursue an agenda focused on various innovative and eco-friendly practices aimed at minimizing its environmental footprint, primarily in the construction materials sector.

### Focus on Carbon Neutrality

A central goal for Saint-Gobain is achieving carbon neutrality by 2050. This ambitious target not only aims to align with international sustainability goals but also commits the organization to significantly reduce greenhouse gas emissions associated with the built environment. To support this goal, the company has set specific targets for carbon emission reductions by 2035-2040, underlining a strategic emphasis on decarbonization efforts.

### Sustainable Construction Solutions

Saint-Gobain prioritizes its leadership in sustainable construction by integrating innovative materials and practices into its core strategy. The focus includes developing sustainable and efficient building materials, such as Low-Carbon Concrete and Glass Fuel Insulation. These innovations aim to enhance resource efficiency while addressing the need for energy conservation within the construction sector. Research on next-generation manufacturing technologies and carbon capture techniques is also vital, as these will contribute to minimizing production-related emissions and shaping industry standards on emissions management.

### Energy Efficiency Enhancements

Improving energy efficiency is a significant aspect of the R&D strategy at Saint-Gobain. The organization aims to enhance the energy performance metrics of buildings through innovations related to retrofitting practices and energy-efficient building materials. By focusing on energy-efficient renovations and promoting energy conservation, the company seeks to improve the operational costs and overall sustainability of building designs throughout their lifecycle.

### Innovative Materials and Waste Reduction

Innovation extends to the development of advanced materials that not only meet environmental standards but also cater to consumer demand for sustainable products. Saint-Gobain is actively engaged in initiatives related to waste reduction and resource recycling, reflecting the broader industry trend towards operating responsibly within environmental constraints. The emphasis on a circular economy highlights the organization's commitment to eliminating waste and reusing resources as part of its operational model.

### Collaborations and Knowledge Transfer

Moreover, Saint-Gobain emphasizes collaboration with educational institutions and industry partners to foster innovation in sustainable architecture and engineering. This approach enhances knowledge transfer and supports the development of a skilled workforce ready to adopt sustainable practices. The organization recognizes the importance of interdisciplinary collaboration to leverage diverse expertise in addressing sustainability challenges effectively.

### Conclusion

In summary, Saint-Gobain's research and development priorities revolve around sustainability, carbon neutrality, energy efficiency, innovative materials, and strategic collaborations. These focused efforts are essential for not only advancing the company's competitive position but also for making significant strides towards reducing the environmental impact associated with the construction industry.
