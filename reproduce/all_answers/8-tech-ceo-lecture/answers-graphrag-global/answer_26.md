### Integration of Expertise

By leveraging over 90 technical disciplines, Exponent draws knowledge from various sectors, including biology and accident reconstruction. The integration of engineering principles with biological research enables Exponent to utilize advanced testing methods, including physical testing and simulations, contributing to a robust scientific method in biomechanics investigations.

### Advanced Methodologies and Tools

Exponent incorporates tools such as advanced testing devices (e.g., crash test dummies) and computational modeling in their investigations. These methodologies allow for real-world scenario simulations, which are critical for assessing injury risks and understanding injury causation. Additionally, the use of medical imaging technologies, like MRIs and CT scans, facilitates empirical analysis of injury mechanisms and aids in relating technical findings to clinical outcomes.
