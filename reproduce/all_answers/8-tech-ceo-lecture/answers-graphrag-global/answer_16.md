## Saint-Gobain's Approach to Engaging Its Workforce Around Sustainability Goals

Compagnie de Saint-Gobain has developed a comprehensive strategy for engaging and aligning its diverse workforce around its sustainability goals. This approach encompasses various initiatives that integrate sustainability into the corporate culture while promoting collaboration and innovation throughout the organization.

### Leadership Commitment

Under the leadership of CEO Benoît Bazin, Saint-Gobain actively promotes sustainability as a core aspect of its mission. Bazin emphasizes environmental responsibility in his presentations, communicating the strategies implemented to address challenges within the industry. His emphasis fosters a unified direction among the workforce, aligning employees around shared sustainability goals. Such a leadership approach highlights the importance of sustainability, ensuring that all employees are informed and engaged with the organization’s targets.

### Sustainability Goals and Initiatives

Saint-Gobain has established clear Sustainability Goals that serve as guiding principles for its operations across the construction and production sectors. These goals reflect a commitment to sustainable practices, resource efficiency, and carbon neutrality by 2050. The company's initiative to incorporate an Internal Carbon Price aligns operations with sustainability objectives, encouraging employees to consider the economic impact of their decision-making on carbon emissions.

### Employee Engagement and Collaboration

A key aspect of Saint-Gobain’s strategy involves fostering a culture of collaboration and engagement among its 168,000 employees. The organization emphasizes employee alignment with sustainability initiatives through various educational programs, workshops, and collaborative projects. By creating discussion forums and participatory events, Saint-Gobain not only informs employees about the importance of sustainability but also empowers them to contribute actively towards achieving these shared objectives.

### Innovative Practices and Knowledge Sharing

Saint-Gobain's commitment to sustainability is linked to innovative practices that enhance energy efficiency and reduce environmental impact. The company prioritizes interdisciplinary collaboration within its research and development initiatives, ensuring that employees from various fields work together to tackle sustainability challenges. This collaborative spirit is further reinforced by partnerships with academic institutions, such as Columbia University, which facilitates knowledge transfer and professional development related to sustainability.

### Conclusion

In summary, Saint-Gobain’s approach to engaging its diverse workforce around sustainability goals is underpinned by strong leadership commitment, well-defined sustainability initiatives, and a collaborative work culture. By aligning its employees with its sustainability objectives and fostering an environment of innovation and shared responsibility, Saint-Gobain is not only enhancing its competitive edge but also contributing positively to environmental stewardship within the construction industry.
