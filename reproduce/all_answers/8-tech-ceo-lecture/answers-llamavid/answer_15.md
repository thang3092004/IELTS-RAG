Tech is a leadership principle at Saint-Gobain, which emphasizes the importance of innovation, technology, and engineering in the company's strategy. The CEO of Saint-Gobain, Benoit Bazen, has made it a priority to implement this principle by focusing on innovation through research and development, as well as scaling up existing innovations. For example, Saint-Gobain has invested in new technologies like 3D printing to create more efficient and sustainable building materials. The company has also been working on developing new materials like biomass gypsum wallboard and lightweight concrete for buildings, which can reduce emissions and energy use. By investing in research and scaling up existing innovations, Saint-Gobain has been able to create products that have a positive impact on the environment and society.
