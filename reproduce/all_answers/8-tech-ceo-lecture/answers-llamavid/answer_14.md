Saint-Gobain addresses the financial barriers homeowners face by providing financing solutions through partnerships with financial institutions. For example, in France, homeowners can get a loan of up to €70000 for energy-efficient renovations. This helps them pay for the cost of the improvements without having to take the money out of pocket. In addition, Saint-Gobain has a program called "Energie Plus" which provides up to 30% of the cost of energy-efficient renovations as a loan, spread over 10 years at an interest rate of 2.85%. This helps homeowners afford the upfront costs and save energy in the long run. The company also offers free energy audits and advice to help homeowners understand their energy use and identify potential energy savings. By providing financing solutions, advice, and audits, Saint-Gobain is making energy-efficient renovations more accessible and affordable for homeowners.
