Saint-Gobain has taken a holistic approach to engaging its diverse workforce around its sustainability goals. The company has defined its purpose as "Grow and Impact" and aligned its strategy around this purpose. It has created a cross-functional group called Tech for Sustainability, which includes representatives from R&D, operations, engineering, and other departments. This group is responsible for developing innovations that minimize the company's carbon emissions. Saint-Gobain has also launched a program called Tech for Impact, which aims to increase the number of women employees by 100%. The company has also created a digital platform to connect its employees with projects around the world. By involving its employees in the development of innovations and the implementation of sustainability projects, Saint-Gobain is able to leverage their expertise and passion to drive meaningful progress towards its goals.
