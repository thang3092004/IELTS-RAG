According to Rajesh Jain, building a strong company culture requires two main things:

1. Clearly defined purpose and vision: Having a clear sense of purpose and vision helps align everyone around a common goal. This gives employees a sense of meaning and direction. 

2. Emphasis on innovation: Innovation is key to staying competitive and driving growth. Companies that invest in R&D and new ideas are more likely to succeed.

Rajesh believes that companies should have a culture that encourages innovation and experimentation, while also ensuring that employees have a clear sense of purpose and direction. A strong company culture is one that is built on shared values and a common goal, which helps employees feel motivated and aligned with the company's mission.
