Saint-Gobain has a commitment to carbon neutrality, aiming to be carbon neutral by 2050. The company has a strategy called "Grow and Impact" which aims to maximize the impact of its solutions in buildings and other construction projects. It aims to achieve this by developing products and solutions that reduce emissions, as well as creating innovations to increase energy efficiency and use of renewable materials. In 2017, Saint-Gobain set a target of reducing its carbon emissions by 2 billion euros in turnover by 2030. The company has already made progress in this area, with a 20% increase in energy efficiency and 30% increase in the share of turnover from low-carbon products. Saint-Gobain also aims to reduce its carbon emissions from its buildings by 100% by 2030.
