A 'profcon' is a term used to describe a company that prioritizes profitability and sustainable growth over rapid expansion and high valuations, which are typical characteristics of a unicorn startup. Unlike unicorns, which are privately held startups valued at over $1 billion and often focus on scaling quickly, profcons emphasize steady revenue generation, cost management, and long-term viability. The key difference lies in their business approach: profcons aim for financial stability and consistent profits, while unicorns often prioritize market share and growth potential, sometimes at the expense of immediate profitability.
