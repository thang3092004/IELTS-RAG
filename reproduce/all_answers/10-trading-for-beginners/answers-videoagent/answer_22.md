A trading journal can be used to assess the effectiveness of a chosen target placement strategy by recording each trade's entry and exit points, the rationale behind the target placement, and the outcome of the trade. By reviewing this data over time, a trader can identify patterns, evaluate whether the target placement strategy consistently meets their goals, and make adjustments as needed to improve performance. The journal helps in analyzing the success rate of hitting targets, the risk-reward ratio, and the impact of market conditions on the strategy's effectiveness
