It is considered unwise to solely rely on trading strategies obtained from external sources because these strategies may not be tailored to your specific financial situation, risk tolerance, or investment goals. Additionally, external strategies may not account for changing market conditions or unforeseen events. Relying solely on them can lead to significant financial losses if they are not thoroughly understood or if they are based on outdated or incorrect information. It is important to conduct your own research and analysis and consider seeking advice from a qualified financial advisor.
