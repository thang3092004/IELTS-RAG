*   **Use Exponential Moving Averages (EMAs) to Predict the Market's Direction.** EMAs can indicate whether the trend is likely to continue. The sources recommend using 3 EMAs&#x20;8, 20, and 50. When all three are aligned in the direction of the trend (i.e., 8 below 20 below 50 in a bearish trend), the probability is high that the market will continue to move in that direction. When the EMAs cross, the signal is no longer valid, so a trader should not enter a trade.
