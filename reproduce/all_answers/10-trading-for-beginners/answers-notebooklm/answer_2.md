The instructor in the source suggests that it **usually takes between 12 and 18 months to become a consistently profitable trader**. This is based on his experience working with thousands of traders over many years.  He breaks down the 18 months into three periods:

*   **6 months to learn the concepts of trading**.
*   **6–12 months for demo trading or paper trading to test your skills, tweak them and go live**.
*   **18 months to fine-tune the trading skills and build confidence to become a consistently profitable trader**.

The instructor emphasizes that **learning to trade is like learning any other skill**, such as playing an instrument or golf, and **requires time and effort**. He also notes that **it is important to have an interest in trading and a desire to succeed** in order to make the time investment worthwhile.
