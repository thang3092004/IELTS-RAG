Strategies and techniques that enable consistent trading success include:

1. Developing a solid understanding of technical analysis and price action patterns. This includes understanding candlestick patterns, support and resistance levels, and identifying trend phases.

2. Having a systematic approach to identifying high probability trading setups based on your trading rules. This includes using exponential moving averages, pin bars, and other technical indicators.

3. Developing a disciplined approach to entering and exiting trades. This includes setting stop losses and target prices, and managing position size.

4. Managing emotions and avoiding impulsive trades based on fear or greed. This includes journaling your trades to review your performance and make adjustments.

5. Having a long-term perspective and avoiding short-term speculative trades. This includes focusing on wealth creation over short-term gains.

6. Keeping costs low by using a low-cost broker and minimizing fees. This includes understanding the impact of spreads, rollovers, and other costs.

In summary, consistent trading success requires developing a solid technical foundation, using a systematic approach to trading, managing emotions, and keeping costs low. These factors enable a trader to make consistent profits over the long-term.
