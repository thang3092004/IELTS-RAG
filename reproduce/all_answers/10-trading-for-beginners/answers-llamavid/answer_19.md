The purpose of conducting a quarterly review of trading journal entries is to review your performance and identify areas for improvement. By reviewing your journal entries, you can gain insights into your emotional state, performance, and the performance of your trading system. This review can help you determine if you are sticking to your trading plan, if you are making errors, and if your risk management is optimal. The journal review can also help you identify areas where you are leaving money on the table and opportunities to increase your position size. By reviewing your journal entries, you can develop a better understanding of your trading system and make adjustments to improve your performance. This is an important part of the process of becoming a consistently profitable trader.
