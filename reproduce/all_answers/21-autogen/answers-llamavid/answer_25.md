The concept of 'flipping the script' can be applied to understanding the evolution of AI agents by considering the various roles and responsibilities they are taking on. At first, AI agents were mainly used for simple text-based tasks like answering questions and generating text. However, over time, AI agents have evolved to become more complex and capable of performing tasks that require higher-level thinking and problem-solving. They are now able to generate code, write reports, and interact with other agents, among other things. By flipping the script, we can see how the roles and responsibilities of AI agents have evolved from simple text-based tasks to more complex and sophisticated tasks. This demonstrates how AI agents are becoming increasingly capable and powerful.
