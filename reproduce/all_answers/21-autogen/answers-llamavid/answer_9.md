To access bots running in specific roles to obtain desired outputs, users can specify their desired roles when interacting with them. This allows the bots to tailor their responses based on the roles. For example, if a user wants a bot to generate code, they can specify a "Code Generator" role. If a user wants a bot to fetch news, they can specify a "News Retrieval" role. By specifying a role, the bot can then use the knowledge and capabilities associated with that role to provide a response that is most relevant to the user's query.
