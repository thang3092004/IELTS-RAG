3. The LLMs do not have the ability to reason about the text and tasks at the level of the document. They are unable to integrate the information across sentences and paragraphs.

4. The LLMs do not have the ability to identify the main ideas and themes within the text. They are unable to summarize and connect the information across the text.

5. The LLMs do not have the ability to identify the key concepts and ideas within the text. They are unable to organize the information in a logical manner.
