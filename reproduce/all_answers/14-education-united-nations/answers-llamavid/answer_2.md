Education Cannot Wait's model facilitates its reach by partnering with various stakeholders, including governments, local communities, and private sector companies. This collaboration allows them to leverage resources and expertise to provide quality education to children in crisis situations. For example, they work with governments to improve school infrastructure, such as building new schools and classrooms. They also partner with local communities and civil society organizations to identify the needs of children and develop education programs. They work with private sector companies like Microsoft to provide digital learning resources and connectivity. By leveraging these resources and partnerships, Education Cannot Wait is able to provide the necessary support to reach children in the most challenging circumstances.
