The LEGO Foundation has contributed to efforts to improve education for children in crisis and emergency settings in several ways. For example, it has partnered with UNICEF to support the Education Cannot Wait initiative, which aims to provide quality education to millions of children in countries affected by conflict, violence, and displacement. The LEGO Foundation has also supported initiatives to improve teacher training and provide resources like computers and textbooks to help children in crisis settings. In addition, the LEGO Foundation has supported initiatives to improve education for children in emergency settings like refugee camps. These efforts aim to help children in crisis situations have the opportunity to learn and gain skills that will help them build a better future.
