Girls in Afghanistan face numerous challenges in accessing secondary education, including a lack of resources, teacher training, and safe learning spaces. Education Cannot Wait has taken several concrete steps to address these issues. For example, it has mobilized over $1.5 billion in funding for education in Afghanistan, including investments in teacher training, learning materials, and mental health services. It has also worked closely with Afghanistan's Ministry of Education to develop a plan to ensure that girls in conflict-affected areas can safely access education. In addition, Education Cannot Wait has supported girls' education in countries like Pakistan, where the Taliban has banned girls from attending school. By providing the resources and training necessary to support girls' education, Education Cannot Wait is helping to bridge the gap in access to quality education for girls in Afghanistan and other countries.
