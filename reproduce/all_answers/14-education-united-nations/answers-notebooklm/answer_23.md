Education Cannot Wait (ECW) is a global fund that aims to bridge the gap between humanitarian aid and development assistance in providing education in crisis-affected contexts. Here are some ways how ECW works:

*   **Joint Programming and Collaboration:** ECW emphasizes joint programming, bringing together various UN agencies, civil society organizations, local communities, and host governments to collaborate on a single education program led by the Ministry of Education in the affected country. This approach avoids competition and promotes a holistic, child-centered approach. 
*   **Focus on the Most Vulnerable:** ECW prioritizes reaching children and youth in the most challenging circumstances, such as conflict zones, refugee camps, and areas affected by climate-induced disasters. They have reached children in militia-controlled areas of the Central African Republic, Syria, Mali, Chad, the Democratic Republic of Congo, and even Afghanistan.
*   **Holistic Quality Education:** ECW provides a holistic education that goes beyond basic literacy and numeracy. Their programs include teacher training, mental health and psychosocial services, cash transfers for families living in extreme poverty, school feeding programs, learning materials and textbooks, clean water and sanitation facilities. They also focus on gender parity, removing barriers to girls’ education, and promoting peace-building and human rights.  
*   **Bridging the Funding Gap:** ECW raises funds from various sources, including member states, the private sector, and high-net-worth individuals, to address the funding gap for education in emergencies. By mobilizing resources and advocating for increased investment in education, they ensure that education is not neglected during crises.  

**Examples of ECW’s approach:**

*   In the Central African Republic, where multiple militia groups operate, ECW, through its partners, reached children with education programs.
*   ECW provided psychosocial support to Rohingya refugee children in Cox’s Bazar, Bangladesh. These services helped the children process their trauma and perform better academically. 

ECW's approach to providing education in crisis settings goes beyond a short-term fix. **By investing in a holistic, collaborative, and sustainable model, ECW contributes to long-term development goals and helps children build a better future.**
