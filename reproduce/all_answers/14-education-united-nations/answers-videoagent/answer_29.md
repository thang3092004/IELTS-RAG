Education can play a crucial role in promoting peace and social cohesion in societies affected by conflict and division by fostering understanding, tolerance, and respect among individuals. It can provide a platform for dialogue and reconciliation by teaching conflict resolution skills and encouraging critical thinking. Education can also help to address the root causes of conflict by promoting social and economic development, reducing inequalities, and empowering marginalized groups. By creating inclusive and equitable educational environments, societies can build a foundation for lasting peace and unity.
