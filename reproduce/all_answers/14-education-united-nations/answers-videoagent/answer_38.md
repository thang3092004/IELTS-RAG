Education can contribute to promoting global citizenship and a sense of shared responsibility for the future of the planet by fostering awareness and understanding of global issues, such as climate change, poverty, and inequality. It encourages critical thinking and empathy, enabling individuals to appreciate diverse perspectives and cultures. Education also empowers people with the skills and knowledge needed to engage in sustainable practices and advocate for policies that protect the environment and promote social justice. By instilling values of cooperation, respect, and responsibility, education helps individuals recognize their role in the global community and motivates them to take action for a more equitable and sustainable world.
