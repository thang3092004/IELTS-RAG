Research and innovation play a crucial role in driving progress towards achieving quality education for all. Research helps in understanding the challenges and barriers to education, such as socio-economic factors, access to resources, and learning disabilities. It provides evidence-based insights that inform policy-making and educational practices. Innovation, on the other hand, introduces new methods, technologies, and approaches to teaching and learning. It can lead to the development of digital learning platforms, personalized learning experiences, and inclusive educational materials that cater to diverse learning needs. Together, research and innovation contribute to creating more effective, equitable, and accessible education systems, ultimately helping to achieve the goal of quality education for all.
