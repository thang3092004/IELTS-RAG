Girls in Afghanistan face several challenges in accessing secondary education, including cultural barriers, security concerns, poverty, and lack of infrastructure. The Taliban's restrictions on female education have further exacerbated these challenges. Education Cannot Wait (ECW) has addressed these challenges by providing funding and support for educational programs that focus on girls' education, building safe learning environments, and working with local communities to promote the importance of education for girls. ECW also collaborates with other organizations to ensure that educational resources reach the most marginalized children, including girls in conflict-affected areas.
