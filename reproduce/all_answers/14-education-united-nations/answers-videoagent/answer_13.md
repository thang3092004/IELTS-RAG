The crisis in foundational learning affects education systems globally by hindering students' ability to acquire basic skills such as literacy and numeracy, which are essential for further learning and personal development. This crisis can lead to increased dropout rates, lower educational attainment, and reduced economic opportunities. To address this issue, education systems can implement measures such as improving teacher training, adopting evidence-based teaching methods, increasing access to quality educational resources, and ensuring equitable education opportunities for all students. Additionally, leveraging technology to provide personalized learning experiences and engaging communities in supporting education can also be effective steps.
