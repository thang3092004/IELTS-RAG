Parents and families play a crucial role in supporting children's education and fostering lifelong learning. They provide emotional support, create a conducive learning environment at home, and set expectations for educational achievement. Parents can engage in their children's education by helping with homework, attending school events, and communicating with teachers. They also serve as role models for lifelong learning by demonstrating curiosity, reading, and pursuing their own educational interests. Additionally, families can encourage a love for learning by exposing children to diverse experiences, such as visiting museums, participating in cultural activities, and encouraging exploration and creativity.
