The United Nations plays a crucial role in coordinating efforts to achieve Sustainable Development Goal 4 (SDG4), which aims to ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all. The UN, through its specialized agencies like UNESCO, provides a platform for international cooperation and dialogue, sets global standards and benchmarks, and mobilizes resources and partnerships to support countries in implementing educational policies and programs. The UN also monitors progress towards SDG4, collects and disseminates data, and provides technical assistance and capacity-building to help countries overcome challenges in achieving quality education for all.
