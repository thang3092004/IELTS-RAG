1. **Governments can:**

- Increase funding for education to improve teacher salaries and working conditions, making the profession more attractive.

- Provide scholarships and financial incentives for students to pursue teaching degrees.

- Implement policies to reduce class sizes, allowing teachers to give more individual attention to students.

- Invest in teacher training and professional development programs to enhance teaching skills and methodologies.

- Encourage alternative certification programs to attract professionals from other fields into teaching.



2. **Teachers can:**

- Engage in continuous professional development to stay updated with the latest teaching methods and technologies.

- Collaborate with other teachers to share resources and best practices.

- Utilize technology to enhance learning experiences and reach a wider range of students.

- Foster an inclusive classroom environment that supports diverse learning needs. 

Governments can increase funding, provide incentives, reduce class sizes, invest in training, and encourage alternative certification. Teachers can engage in professional development, collaborate, use technology, and foster inclusivity.
