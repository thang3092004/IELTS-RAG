Tokamak Energy LLC leverages the spherical tokamak design in conjunction with high-temperature superconductors (HTS) to achieve improved physics performance and cost efficiency in its approach to fusion.

Here's a breakdown of how the spherical tokamak design contributes to Tokamak Energy LLC's strategy:

*   **Reduced Magnet Usage**: Compared to traditional tokamaks, the spherical design requires fewer magnets, leading to significant savings in capital expenditure, as magnets constitute a major portion of the overall cost.
*   **Enhanced Physics Performance**: The spherical design facilitates improved physics performance through a phenomenon known as bootstrap current, allowing for greater current leverage within the fusion process.

Tokamak Energy LLC's focus on **HTS magnets** further enhances the benefits of the spherical tokamak:

*   **High Current Capacity**: HTS magnets can withstand tremendous currents without losing superconductivity, a critical aspect for efficient fusion reactions.

By combining these elements, Tokamak Energy LLC aims to accelerate the development of commercially viable fusion energy.
