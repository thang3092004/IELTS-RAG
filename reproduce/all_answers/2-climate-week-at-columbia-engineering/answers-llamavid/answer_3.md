The Department of Energy's Milestone program is a competitive program designed to accelerate the development of promising energy technologies. It provides funding to support research and development of advanced energy projects that have the potential to achieve significant breakthroughs in the energy field. The program is open to both industry and academic partners, with the goal of helping companies bring their concepts to the construction-ready stage. It is designed to support projects that have the potential to make a significant impact on the energy landscape, such as deploying fusion energy or developing new solar technologies. The Milestone program is seen as an important step towards bringing advanced energy technologies to market, and it has been instrumental in helping to launch many of the companies that are working on the cutting edge of the energy industry.
