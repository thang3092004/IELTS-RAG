The challenges of transitioning from charcoal to LPG as a cooking fuel in Ghana include the initial cost of LPG equipment, limited access to LPG supply in rural areas, and cultural preferences for traditional cooking methods. Additionally, there may be a lack of awareness and education about the benefits of LPG. The potential benefits of using LPG include reduced indoor air pollution, which can improve health outcomes, decreased deforestation and environmental impact, and greater efficiency and convenience in cooking.
