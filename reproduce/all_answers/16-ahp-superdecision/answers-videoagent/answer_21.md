The 'inconsistency report' feature in SuperDecisions is used to identify inconsistencies in pairwise comparison judgments by analyzing the consistency ratio of the comparisons. When users make pairwise comparisons to prioritize criteria or alternatives, the software calculates a consistency ratio to determine how consistent the judgments are. If the ratio exceeds a certain threshold, it indicates potential inconsistencies. The report helps users identify which comparisons are inconsistent, allowing them to review and adjust their judgments to improve consistency and ensure more reliable decision-making outcomes.
