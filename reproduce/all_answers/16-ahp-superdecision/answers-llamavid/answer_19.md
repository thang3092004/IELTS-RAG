SuperDecisions provides two main visualization options for displaying sensitivity analysis results:

1. Bar Chart: This shows the priorities of the criteria and alternatives as bars side by side, with the heights proportional to their weights. The priority of each alternative is indicated by a line that starts from the goal node and ends at the last alternative node. 

2. Scatter Plot: This shows the priorities as dots on a graph, with the priority of each alternative plotted as a dot. The priority of each alternative is indicated by a line that starts from the goal node and ends at the last alternative node.

Both visualizations allow users to easily compare the priorities of the criteria and alternatives. The bar chart provides a more detailed and structured view, while the scatter plot provides a more intuitive and flexible visualization.
