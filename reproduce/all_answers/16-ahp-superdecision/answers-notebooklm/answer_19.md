SuperDecisions offers a variety of visualization options for displaying sensitivity analysis results, allowing for a comprehensive understanding of the model's robustness. These options include:

*   **Plot Chart:** This chart, often used in research papers, visually represents **rank reversals**, points where the ranking of alternatives changes as the weight of a criterion is varied. It shows the weight thresholds at which these reversals occur.
*   **Bar Chart:** The bar chart provides a dynamic view of how the priorities of alternatives change as the weight of a criterion is adjusted. The height of each bar represents the priority of the corresponding alternative. By dragging a pointer, users can observe the **dynamic changes in priorities** in real time.
*   **Pie Chart:** Although not elaborated upon in the sources, SuperDecisions includes a pie chart visualization option for sensitivity analysis.
*   **Horizontal Bar Chart:** This option offers an alternative view of the sensitivity analysis results, similar to the bar chart but with a horizontal orientation.

These visualization tools offer different perspectives on the sensitivity of the model, enabling users to identify critical criteria, assess the impact of weight changes, and gain insights into the stability of their decision-making process.
