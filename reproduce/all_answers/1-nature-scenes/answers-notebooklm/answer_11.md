The sources describe how saddleback clownfish and carpet anemones engage in a mutually beneficial relationship. **The clownfish clean the anemones and protect their eggs, while the anemones provide shelter to the clownfish from predators thanks to their poisonous tentacles.** The clownfish are immune to the poison and can therefore safely shelter within the anemone's tentacles.  This relationship ensures the well-being of both species.
