Humboldt penguins in Peru face a challenging obstacle course when trying to reach the sea for food. Their nesting ground is separated from the ocean by the remains of an old nesting ground and a beach crowded with sea lions.

*   **The old nesting ground is described as a "minefield" that the penguins must cross.**
*   **The beach is crowded with sea lions who are raising their young and "don't like being disturbed".**

To navigate this difficult terrain, the penguins rely on a **"bold and courageous leader" to guide them through.** This leader helps the penguins push through the crowded beach, even resorting to "crowd surfing" on the sea lions when necessary. Despite the challenges, the penguins persevere, and a "cleansing bathe in the ocean" awaits them at the end of their journey.
