Being able to tell in-depth stories in a 'bite-sized' media environment is significant as it allows journalists to provide the public with a quick and accessible way to stay informed about current events. With the rise of social media and short-form video, the demand for quick, digestible news has increased. This has forced journalists to find creative ways to deliver complex information in a concise manner. Short-form video allows journalists to get their news out quickly and efficiently, while still conveying important details. It also allows them to use visuals and sound to enhance their reporting. However, it requires journalists to be skilled at telling compelling stories in a limited amount of time. The ability to do so is important for journalists to remain relevant and impactful in the ever-changing media landscape.
