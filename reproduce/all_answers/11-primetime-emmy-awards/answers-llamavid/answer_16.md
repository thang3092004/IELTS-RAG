The acceptance speech by John Oliver on behalf of the writing team for 'Last Week Tonight' highlights the crucial role of various departments in television production. He specifically mentions the show's writing team, production team, and the crew. He says "We want to thank our executive producers, Scott Henderson and Liz Stanton, and our wonderful team of writers. We also want to thank HBO for not canceling us over the last decade. We also want to thank our amazing crew, who work so hard and get so wet by the Scottish weather. We're so grateful for their unwavering support." This shows how the writing team and production team work together to create the show, while the crew works tirelessly behind the scenes. The speech also acknowledges the importance of the crew's hard work and the show's viewers, showing how all departments contribute to the success of a TV program.
