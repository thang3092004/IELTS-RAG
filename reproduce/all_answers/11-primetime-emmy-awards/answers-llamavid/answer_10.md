Amy J. Johnson's work exemplifies the kind of positivity Karapetyan discusses in that it is focused on highlighting the stories of individuals who have overcome significant adversity. Her documentary 'A Doll Like Me' follows the journey of a young girl with dwarfism as she undergoes a life-changing surgery to increase her height. Throughout the film, Johnson's camera focuses on the emotional moments and personal struggles of the girl as she navigates the surgery and the recovery process. The documentary also includes interviews with the girl's doctors and family members who provide insights into the challenges she faced and how the surgery has impacted her life. The film concludes with a scene showing the girl's newfound confidence and joy, as she is seen running and smiling, reflecting the positive impact of the surgery on her life. Johnson's work highlights the resilience and determination of individuals who have faced adversity, while also celebrating the power of hope and the human spirit to overcome.
