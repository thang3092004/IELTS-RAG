Local news stations can engage viewers and earn their trust by focusing on **truth and facts** and adapting to how viewers consume news.  

* Giselle Fernandez, the host of the 74th Los Angeles Area Emmy Awards, stated that viewers frequently express the need to disconnect from news for their mental well-being due to its distressing nature. However, she emphasized that news avoidance is not a viable solution.  
* Instead, news providers need to explore innovative approaches to **engage viewers and foster trust to maintain their viewership**. This is crucial because the truth and accuracy of information are paramount, particularly in the current climate.
* Fernandez highlighted **local news as a reliable source of trust**.  
* She acknowledged the diverse landscape of the news industry, encompassing **cable, digital platforms, radio, and podcasts**.  
* Regardless of the medium, **storytelling remains the core element**, with a focus on delivering compelling and meaningful narratives to educate viewers. 

Fernandez concluded that, whether or not individuals receive awards, their commitment to delivering the news daily makes them victors.
