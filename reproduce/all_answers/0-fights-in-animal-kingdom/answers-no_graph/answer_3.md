### Defensive Strategies of Musk Oxen  
The musk oxen's defensive formation plays a pivotal role in their survival against predators like wolves. When faced with an approaching wolf, musk oxen instinctively huddle together, forming a protective barrier. This behavior is not merely instinctual but a strategic response to threats. Their thick, dark coats and formidable horns serve as natural armor, enhancing their defense. The effectiveness of this formation is evident in encounters where the collective strength of the group deters lone wolves from attacking. The huddling behavior, characterized by facing the threat as a unified front, significantly increases their chances of survival during such encounters [1].

### Impact of Environmental Conditions  
The harsh environmental conditions of the tundra significantly influence the behaviors and survival strategies of both wolves and musk oxen. The snowy landscape presents challenges that shape the hunting strategies of wolves, who are apex predators adapted to these conditions. Wolves often hunt in packs, utilizing their agility and speed to navigate the treacherous terrain. The snowy environment not only affects their hunting tactics but also impacts the musk oxen's survival strategies. The musk oxen's ability to adapt to these conditions, such as forming defensive formations, is crucial for their survival against the relentless pursuit of wolves [2].
