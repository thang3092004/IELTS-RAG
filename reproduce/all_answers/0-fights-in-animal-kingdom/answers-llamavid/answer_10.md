Mother bears face several challenges when securing salmon. First, they must compete with other bears and even other predators for the salmon, as salmon are a major food source for bears. They must also navigate the dangerous waters and coastal terrain to catch and kill the salmon. The salmon themselves are very difficult to catch and are constantly on the move, making it challenging for the bears to track them. The bears have to be highly skilled hunters to successfully catch a salmon. Finally, the bears have to be very patient and persistent, as salmon runs can last for weeks before the salmon start returning to the river again. The video shows the mother bear's persistence and determination in her pursuit of the salmon, despite the challenges she faces.
