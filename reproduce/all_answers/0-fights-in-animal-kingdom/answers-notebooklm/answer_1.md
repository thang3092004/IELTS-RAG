The sources do not describe how chimpanzees communicate and coordinate their roles as drivers, blockers, and ambushers while hunting. The sources do describe these roles:

* One chimpanzee acts as the **driver**, pushing the prey ahead.
* Other chimpanzees act as **blockers**, taking up positions on either side of the prey so it cannot change direction.
* Other chimpanzees act as **chasers**, attempting to grab the prey.
* One chimpanzee takes up a position ahead of the prey to **ambush** it.
