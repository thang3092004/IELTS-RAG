The otters engage with the caiman because it poses a threat to their young. The caiman wandered into the middle of the otter clan and became trapped by over 20 of them. Although the otter cubs kept their distance at first, the caiman’s refusal to back down caused the cubs to panic and run into the middle of the fight, putting them in extreme danger. The adult otters then stepped up their assault and ultimately overpowered the caiman, killing it. Two of the cubs did not survive the fight.  The otters' motivation to engage with the caiman was to protect their young and eliminate the threat it posed to their lake.
