During the encounter, the physical strength and defensive capabilities of the muskoxen played a crucial role as they utilized their size to create a barrier against the wolves, exhibiting remarkable resilience in the face of a predatory threat.

### Behavioral Strategies

However, muskoxen are known for their instinctual defensive behaviors; when threatened, they tend to gather closely together to protect weaker members of the herd, such as calves or injured individuals. ### Social Dynamics
