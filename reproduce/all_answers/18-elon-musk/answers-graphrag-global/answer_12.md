### DJ Seo's Expertise at Neuralink

DJ Seo is prominently known for his work at Neuralink, where he plays a crucial role in the development of brain-computer interface technologies. His expertise centers around the interface of advanced neural technologies, which may have significant implications for enhancing human capabilities.

One of the key areas of his focus involves the development of flexible electrodes designed to improve the functionality and longevity of brain implants. These electrodes are engineered to minimize immune response, allowing for better integration with brain tissue. This integration is vital, as it enhances the overall effectiveness of brain-computer interfaces, which have the potential to transform our approach to neurotechnology.

In summary, DJ Seo's contributions at Neuralink revolve primarily around the advancement of brain-computer interfaces, particularly through the creation of technology that aims to enhance the interaction between human brains and machines while addressing the challenges presented by immune responses and tissue integration.
