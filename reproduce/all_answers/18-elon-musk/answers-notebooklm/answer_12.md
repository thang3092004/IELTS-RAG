DJ Seo is the co-founder, president, and COO of Neuralink.  His area of expertise is **designing and engineering electrical solutions for biological systems, particularly in the realm of miniaturizing implantable systems.** He worked on ultrasound technology during his PhD, specifically on a system called **"neural dust,"** which involves parking very small computational devices next to neurons. He was drawn to this field because he was interested in understanding the purpose of things and how they were engineered.
