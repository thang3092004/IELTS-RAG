The first several years of Neuralink are focused on solving **basic neurological damage**, such as neuron damage in the spinal cord, neck, or brain. Neuralink aims to assist those with **complete or near-complete loss of connection from the brain to the body**, with Stephen Hawking cited as an example. Other issues the technology seeks to address include:

*   Blindness by stimulating neurons in the visual cortex.
*   Schizophrenia.
*   Seizures.
*   Memory problems.

Elon Musk emphasizes that the initial focus will be on these fundamental neurological issues for the first several years. The idea is to start with simpler applications before moving to more complex ones, similar to needing literacy before tackling “Lord of the Rings”.  This approach is deemed sensible, as it allows Neuralink to address pressing medical needs and gather valuable data before expanding into non-medical applications.
