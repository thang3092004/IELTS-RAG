Elon Musk believes Neuralink has the potential to enhance vision beyond human capabilities. Here are some capabilities he envisions: 

*   **Higher Resolution than Human Eyes**: Musk believes it's possible to achieve vision with higher resolution than human eyes by stimulating neurons in the visual cortex with Neuralink. He explained this by saying that although the number of electrodes might be limited, the electric field between neurons can be adjusted to create patterns, effectively increasing the perceived resolution.  

*   **Seeing in Different Wavelengths**: Musk believes that Neuralink can enable people to see in different wavelengths, such as infrared and ultraviolet, much like the character Geordi La Forge from *Star Trek*. He envisions this as being possible by connecting an external camera to Neuralink, allowing it to receive and process wavelengths beyond the visible light spectrum. 

*   **Object Detection**: While not explicitly mentioned by Musk, DJ Seo, Neuralink’s co-founder, president, and COO,  highlighted the possibility of incorporating object detection algorithms into Neuralink.  This would provide real-time information about the environment, assisting users in navigating their surroundings.  

These enhancements would primarily be aimed at restoring sight for blind people but could eventually be available for augmentation in people without disabilities.
