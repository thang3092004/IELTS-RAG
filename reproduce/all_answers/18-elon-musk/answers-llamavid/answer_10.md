Elon Musk envisions Neuralink providing vision enhancement capabilities that allow users to see with much higher resolution than their current vision. He explains that Neuralink could potentially enable users to see in different wavelengths, like ultraviolet and infrared, and in the future, enable users to see in the same wavelengths that bats and other animals see. This would allow users to see things that are invisible to the human eye, like the building blocks of DNA and other structures in the cell. Musk also envisions Neuralink being able to restore vision to those who have lost their sight, potentially allowing them to see at much higher resolution than before. He notes that this would require implanting additional neurons in the visual cortex and training a neural model to map the signals to the right locations. Overall, Musk sees Neuralink as a way to significantly improve human vision, enabling users to see things that are currently impossible with their current eyes.
