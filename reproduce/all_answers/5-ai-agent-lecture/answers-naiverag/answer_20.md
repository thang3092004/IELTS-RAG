### Basic Editing Features

1. **Voiceover Customization**:
   - Users can change the voiceover of the generated video. For instance, you can switch from a female voice to a male voice, ensuring that the narration fits the desired tone of the content.

2. **Background Music Adjustment**:
   - InVideo AI allows users to remove or replace the background music included in the original video. This feature is particularly useful for users who want a specific audio experience that complements their visuals.

3. **Caption and Text Editing**:
   - The platform supports automatic caption additions, and users can edit these captions if they wish to correct errors or modify text for better clarity. This ensures that the messaging is clear and tailored to the audience.

### Advanced Editing Options

4. **Media Replacement**:
   - Users can access a media library containing various stock images and clips. They have the option to replace existing footage with new selections from this library, enhancing the visual appeal of their project.

5. **Frame Modification**:

6. **Customization of Templates**:
   - The editing software often provides a variety of templates for different types of videos, such as YouTube content, social media reels, etc. Users can customize these templates according to their preferences.
