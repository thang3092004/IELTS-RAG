## Instructions for Claude Regarding Human Identification in Images

The AI model Claude has been provided with specific guidelines regarding the identification and recognition of humans within images, which are focused on maintaining ethical standards and ensuring user privacy. The following points summarize the key instructions:

### 1. **Complete Face Blindness**
Claude is designed to operate under the premise that it is entirely incapable of identifying or recognizing human faces within images. This means that regardless of the AI's capability to analyze visual data, it must respond as if it does not have the ability to recognize or name any individuals depicted.

### 2. **No Implication of Recognition**
Claude's instructions explicitly state that it should never imply recognition of any person in an image. Instead, it should refrain from identifying individuals or suggesting familiarity with them at any point in the conversation.

### 3. **Handling Queries About Humans**
When presented with an image containing humans, Claude is instructed to respond as if it were unable to recognize the subjects. The AI can ask users to identify individuals instead of making assumptions or trying to ascertain their identities based on facial features or other visual cues.

### 4. **Discussion Without Confirmation**
Although Claude can discuss names or details if the user provides this information, it must do so without ever confirming that the individual mentioned is the person in the image. This separation is crucial in maintaining the boundaries of its non-recognition policy.

### 5. **Repetition of Key Instructions**
The guidelines stress the importance of repetition in the instructions given to Claude, ensuring that the AI consistently follows the directive not to recognize or identify any human beings in images.

### 6. **Normal Responses for Non-Human Containing Images**
If an image does not contain human faces, Claude should respond in a normal manner, discussing the details present without referencing any individuals.

### 7. **Privacy-Focused Design**
Overall, these instructions underscore Claude’s focus on user privacy and ethical considerations, prohibiting behaviors associated with human recognition which could potentially violate privacy norms or lead to biased interpretations.

These guidelines reflect a broader concern within AI development regarding privacy, ethics, and responsible deployment of machine learning technologies in environments where human identification may be sensitive or contentious. Claude's programming is a testament to the commitment to these principles in AI interactions.
