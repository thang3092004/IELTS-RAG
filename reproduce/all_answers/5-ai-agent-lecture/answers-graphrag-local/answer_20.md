# Modifying Output Videos in InVideo AI

InVideo AI offers a versatile platform for users interested in creating and modifying videos. The process of customizing the output video generated by InVideo AI involves several interactive features and functionalities that enhance user engagement and content production. Here are some of the key methods through which users can modify their videos:

## Utilizing VideoMaker Features

One of the most significant tools within InVideo AI is **VideoMaker**, which allows users to transform ideas, documents, or scripts into engaging narrated videos. Users can make several modifications during the video creation process, including:

- **Customizing Narration**: Users can choose to change the voiceover for their videos. This feature allows for the selection of different voices or styles, which can include male or female voices, enhancing the audio appeal of the final product.
- **Background Music Adjustments**: The platform enables users to remove or change background music. This flexibility allows for a more tailored audio experience that aligns with the video content better.
- **Automatic Captioning**: InVideo AI includes an automatic caption addition feature, which can be edited post-creation to ensure accuracy and enhance viewer accessibility.

## Editing and Enhancements

The editing interface in InVideo AI supports a range of modifications, including:

- **Template Usage**: Users can select from a variety of templates, aiding in quick video production without needing extensive design skills. These templates can be adjusted to fit specific content needs.
- **B-roll and Stock Footages**: The platform enables the integration of stock images and videos to supplement the primary content. This flexibility in sourcing additional visuals enriches the video narrative and aesthetic.
- **Interactive Elements**: Users can interact with various options presented in the interface, such as adding voiceovers, music selections, and creating future video versions. This interactivity allows users to fine-tune their projects extensively.

## User Guidance and Support

InVideo AI also provides instructional resources to assist users throughout the editing process. Tutorials and guided walkthroughs help users understand how to utilize different features effectively. Moreover, the platform encourages users to explore these capabilities, assuring a smooth transition from concept to finished video.

## Conclusion

In summary, InVideo AI empowers users to modify their output videos with an array of features designed for customization. By leveraging tools like VideoMaker, along with editing and enhancement options, users can create engaging content that is informative and visually appealing. Through intuitive interactions and access to various resources, users can maximize their productivity and creativity in video creation.
