# Models Claiming Superiority Over GPT-4

In the competitive landscape of AI language models, several entities have made claims regarding their performance relative to OpenAI’s GPT-4. The following models have frequently been mentioned in the context of outpacing GPT-4 across various benchmarks:

## Claude 3 Opus

One of the primary competitors discussed in relation to GPT-4 is **Claude 3 Opus**. Various performance evaluations indicate that Claude 3 Opus has demonstrated superior results in specific areas, particularly in tasks involving graduate-level reasoning, GRE/GMAT verbal sections, and certain math data sets. For instance, Claude 3 Opus has outperformed GPT-4 in benchmarks like the GSM 8K test, highlighting its competitiveness against GPT-4 across diverse evaluation scenarios.

## Claude 3.5 Sonnet

Additionally, **Claude 3.5 Sonnet** is positioned as another model that competes directly with GPT-4. It has been noted for its advancements in coding tasks and capabilities involving tool-use scenarios. Benchmark assessments reveal that Claude 3.5 Sonnet not only competes closely with GPT-4 but, in specific contexts, shows performance metrics that exceed those of GPT-4.

## Other Notable Mentions
