### Modifying Output Videos with InVideo AI

InVideo AI is a powerful tool that allows users to generate and customize videos for various platforms such as YouTube, TikTok, Instagram Reels, and more. The process of modifying the output video can be accomplished through a range of features embedded within the application. Here is how users can effectively modify their generated videos:

#### Editing and Customization Options

1. **Voiceover Modifications**:
   Users have the ability to change the voiceover of their generated video. For instance, they can opt to switch to a male voice or choose different accent options, such as a southern accent. This feature allows for personalization to better match the video's theme or the intended audience.

2. **Adding or Removing Background Music**:
   InVideo AI enables users to add background music or even remove it entirely if preferred. This flexibility is crucial for setting the right mood and tone for the video. Users can select from a library of music options, ensuring their video conveys the desired emotional impact.

3. **Incorporating Captions**:
   Automatic captions can be added to enhance accessibility and viewer engagement. Users can edit these captions as necessary, tailoring them to improve clarity and comprehension. Additionally, any specific frames can be highlighted or adjusted to align with the overall narrative.

4. **Editing Individual Frames**:
   Users can dive into finer details by editing individual frames within the video. This includes adjusting text placement, changing visuals, or any element that enhances the story being told. Such granular control allows for a more polished final product.

5. **Incorporating Stock Media**:
   InVideo AI has a feature that facilitates the integration of stock images and stock videos. Users can source relevant B-roll footage that complements their main video content, thus enriching the overall visual experience.

6. **Setting Video Length**:
   Users have the flexibility to set the desired length of their videos. This function is particularly useful for tailoring the content for specific platforms that have varying time restrictions, ensuring the video meets the requirements of each medium.

#### Final Touches

Once users have made the necessary modifications, they can preview their video to see how changes come together. Users are encouraged to ensure all adjustments enhance the narrative and achieve a cohesive final output. 

In summary, InVideo AI provides a comprehensive suite of editing tools that empowers users to modify generated videos extensively. From voiceovers and backgrounds to detailed frame edits and integration of stock elements, the platform allows extensive customization to meet the user’s creative vision.
