Open-source LLMs like GPT-4 are limited in their ability to handle complex tasks like SWE-Agent. They are more suited for generating text, writing code, and other text-based tasks. The smaller LLMs like Lama 3 and Opus are better at generating text, while the larger models like GPT-4 and GPT-3 are more powerful but still lack the ability to handle complex tasks. This is due to the lack of training data and fine-tuning for specific tasks. SWE-Agent was able to outperform the LLMs in the SWE benchmark, showing that LLMs need to be fine-tuned for specific tasks to be effective.
