### Modifying Output Video in InVideo AI

InVideo AI offers multiple features that allow users to modify output videos according to their specific needs. The flexibility of the platform ensures that users can create engaging video content quickly and efficiently by customizing various elements. Here are some of the primary ways users can modify generated videos:

#### 1. **Editing Voiceovers**
Users can change the voiceover in their videos, which includes options to adjust the voice type or accent. For instance, one can opt for a male voice with a Southern accent or choose a British accent for a more distinctive tone in the narration. This functionality helps tailor the presentation style to better align with the intended audience.

#### 2. **Changing Background Music**
The platform provides the option to add background music or remove existing tracks. This is essential for enhancing the emotional tone of the video or for ensuring that the audio matches the visual content effectively. Users can experiment with different music tracks to find the right fit for their projects.

#### 3. **Incorporating B-Roll Footage**
Users can add B-roll footage to their videos to enhance storytelling and visual appeal. This feature allows for the integration of supplementary clips that provide additional context or richness to the main content, making the video more engaging.

#### 4. **Transitions and Visual Effects**
InVideo AI enables users to apply various transitions between clips, creating smoother visual storytelling. These transitions can significantly improve the overall flow of the video, making it more visually appealing.

#### 5. **Using Templates and Customization**
The platform offers predefined templates that help streamline the video creation process. Users can choose templates suited to their theme or purpose and then customize them by adjusting text, images, and layout.

#### 6. **Previewing Changes**
The "Preview Area" is another crucial aspect where users can see real-time effects of the code or edits made to the video. This enables them to ensure that the modifications align with their vision before finalizing the output.

Overall, InVideo AI's comprehensive editing features allow users to create a highly personalized video experience. By modifying voiceovers, music, visual elements, and overall structure, users can achieve their desired output and effectively engage their target audience.
