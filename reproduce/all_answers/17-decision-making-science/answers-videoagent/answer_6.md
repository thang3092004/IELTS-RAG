The concept of "disrupting the economics of an attack" helps organizations prioritize security investments by focusing on making attacks more costly and less profitable for attackers. By understanding the cost-benefit analysis from an attacker's perspective, organizations can identify and invest in security measures that increase the difficulty, time, and resources required for an attack. This approach helps in allocating resources effectively to areas that will have the most significant impact on reducing the likelihood and impact of potential attacks, thereby optimizing security investments.
