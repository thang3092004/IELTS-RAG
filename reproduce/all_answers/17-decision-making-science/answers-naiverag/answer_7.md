### John Yokim's Early Experiences with Computing

John Yokim's journey into the world of technology and his subsequent career at Microsoft can be traced back to his early experiences with computers during his time at the University of Pittsburgh. Growing up in Pittsburgh and eventually pursuing higher education there, Yokim was confronted with the nascent stages of computing in the early 1990s.

#### Initial Engagement with Technology

At the start of his academic journey, Yokim faced a significant gap in access to personal computers compared to today's standards. In the 1990s, many students did not have computers at their fingertips, and Yokim often found himself needing to visit computer labs for his coursework. This necessity to utilize shared resources ignited an initial spark of interest and a deeper understanding of how computers could facilitate learning. It was through these experiences that he began to appreciate the vital role technology plays in enhancing educational opportunities and simplifying complex tasks, particularly in mathematics.

#### Transition to the IT Field

This formative period at the University of Pittsburgh was characterized by an evolving passion for technology. Yokim pursued a degree that emphasized mathematics, often using computers as tools to aid his studies. His engagement with computing began as a hobby and evolved into a professional aspiration that aligned with his academic background. As he worked on his degree, he took on student employment within the university's computer labs, where he gained practical experience in managing technology and assisting other students. 

#### First Steps into Professional Life

After graduating, Yokim made a calculated decision to merge his passion for technology with his professional ambitions by becoming a technical trainer. His role involved teaching individuals how to effectively utilize computing tools, particularly in a corporate environment. He conducted training sessions that covered various applications, including spreadsheets and internet searches, which were critical at a time when the internet was emerging as a primary tool for business and education.

#### The Microsoft Connection

Yokim's early experiences paved the way for his career at Microsoft. By successfully combining his technical knowledge acquired through education and hands-on experiences with his role as a trainer, he developed skills that made him a desirable candidate for roles within a tech giant like Microsoft. ### Conclusion

In summary, John Yokim's early interactions with computing at the University of Pittsburgh fundamentally shaped his career trajectory, facilitating a transition from a student reliant on computer labs to a seasoned professional in the tech industry.
