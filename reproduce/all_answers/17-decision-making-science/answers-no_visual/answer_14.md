By clearly delineating these stages, Decision Lens allows stakeholders to focus on specific tasks, thereby reducing ambiguity and enhancing clarity in discussions. This structured approach is particularly beneficial in environments where stakeholders may have conflicting priorities, as it provides a common framework for dialogue and negotiation.
