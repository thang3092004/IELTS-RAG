At the University of Pittsburgh, Yokim developed a solid foundation in mathematics and computer science. The university equipped him with critical thinking skills and technical expertise, which were essential as he began his career. #### Usage of Computer Labs

Yokim often utilized computer labs as a student, as personal computers were not as pervasive at the time. This experience of accessing technology in educational environments allowed him to become familiar with various software and computing tools, ultimately enhancing his technical skills. These practical applications of his academic learnings in computer labs helped him gain confidence in his capabilities, setting the stage for his adaptability and proficiency in the technology sector.

#### Professional Development
