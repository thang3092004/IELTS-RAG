In the context of the shift from product-based to service-based models, the Peloton example showcases how companies are moving away from one-time sales to building long-term customer relationships. 

*   While a customer can simply purchase a Peloton bike and use it independently, **the real value lies in the subscription to their driving service**, which offers a more engaging and personalized experience. 
*   This service includes features such as **trainer guidance, stat tracking, achievements, and gamification**, designed to enhance the customer’s enjoyment and encourage continued product use. 
*   This shift highlights how **businesses are focusing on creating ongoing value for customers rather than just selling a physical product.** The emphasis is on providing a holistic experience that keeps customers engaged and fosters loyalty.

This approach is seen in other industries as well, signifying a broader trend in business transformation driven by technology.
