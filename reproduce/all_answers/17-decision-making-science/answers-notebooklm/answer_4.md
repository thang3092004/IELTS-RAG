Decision Lens software addresses the challenge of planning in silos by connecting plans across different time horizons, functions, and levels of an organization. **The software enables integrated planning across an organization to ensure that all levels are working towards a common goal.** For example, when the Navy is running an aircraft carrier group, it can’t plan the airplanes differently from the ships, the power sources, or the base operations. These plans must be integrated to ensure success. Decision Lens allows the Navy to plan across these different functions, connecting the needs of the lowest levels with the strategic objectives of the highest level.
