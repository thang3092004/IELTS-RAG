Decision Lens software is designed to integrate planning across the entire organization, from the highest levels down to the lowest. Traditional planning tools like HP and Excel are designed for silos, focusing on a single function or department. However, this approach is inefficient for organizations like the U.S. Navy with diverse missions and objectives. 

By using Decision Lens, the Navy can connect its planning across all levels, from strategic objectives to tactical plans and execution. This allows for more effective trade-offs and resource allocation. 

The software can also incorporate data from different sources, not just the Navy itself. This helps the Navy make better decisions that align with its overall mission and objectives.

In summary, Decision Lens enables the Navy to move from siloed planning to integrated planning across the entire organization, which is critical for its success.
