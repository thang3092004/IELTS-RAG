Small differences in performance data, such as a tenth of a second in a 40-yard dash, can have a big impact on player evaluation and strategic decisions in professional sports. This can lead to differences in player evaluation and strategic decisions, such as whether to select a player for a certain position or role. The evaluation and strategic decisions made based on this small performance data can impact the outcome of games, championships, and player careers.
