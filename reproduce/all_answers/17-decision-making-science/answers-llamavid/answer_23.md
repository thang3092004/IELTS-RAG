Decision Lens software enables organizations to perform resource optimization by taking into account both strategic value and cost constraints to identify the best mix of investments. The software starts with a hierarchical structure of the organization's priorities, which helps establish a driving set of priorities and objectives. It then uses algorithms to model the trade-offs between different investments and resource scenarios. The software can evaluate the strategic value of different investments, considering factors such as their impact on mission priorities, resource capacity, and scenario planning. 

---

The software also enables organizations to evaluate the cost of different investments, considering factors such as the cost of the investment, resource requirements, and other factors. This helps organizations identify the most cost-effective investments.

---
In summary, Decision Lens software enables organizations to perform resource optimization by evaluating the strategic value and cost of investments, considering both strategic value and cost constraints, and then selecting the best mix of investments. This helps organizations identify the best investments to maximize both strategic value and cost-effectiveness.
