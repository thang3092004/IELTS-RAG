Dan Menicucci is a Chief Security Officer at Microsoft who has over 20 years of experience in cybersecurity. He believes that the key to protecting against cyber threats is understanding the motivations of attackers and being proactive in defending against them. Dan argues that security spend should be directed towards protecting against the most likely forms of attacks, rather than trying to defend against everything. He advocates for investing in AI and machine learning to detect and respond to threats more effectively. Dan also emphasizes the need for agility in the face of rapidly evolving cyber threats, and encourages companies to move to the cloud to gain the benefits of greater agility.
