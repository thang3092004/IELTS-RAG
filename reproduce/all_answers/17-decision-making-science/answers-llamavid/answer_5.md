The concept of 'agility' can be interpreted differently by various stakeholders. For example, to a CIO, agility may mean the ability to quickly move to cloud services and reduce costs. But to an executive, agility could mean the ability to innovate and respond to changing customer needs. Clear terminology is crucial in developing AHP models because it helps stakeholders understand what is meant by key concepts like 'agility' and 'innovation'. Without clear definitions, stakeholders may misunderstand what is being proposed and how it will benefit their organization.
