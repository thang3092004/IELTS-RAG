The Decision Lens software provides a platform for stakeholders with diverse perspectives to collaborate and build consensus during the planning process. 

The software allows stakeholders to easily create and view multiple alternative scenarios and investment options. This enables stakeholders to weigh in on different criteria and provide feedback on the trade-offs between the alternatives.

The software also provides insights and analytics to help stakeholders evaluate the pros and cons of different investments. This helps stakeholders make informed decisions based on their priorities.

In summary, the Decision Lens software facilitates collaboration and consensus-building by enabling stakeholders to view and evaluate alternative scenarios, investments, and criteria in a structured and objective manner.
