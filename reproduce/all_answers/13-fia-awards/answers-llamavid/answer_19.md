The FIA World Rally Championship has adapted to the changing landscape of motorsports by incorporating new events and hybrid technology into its competitions. In recent years, the championship has expanded its calendar to include events in South America and Africa, as well as events that take place in the deserts of the Middle East. This has allowed the championship to showcase the sport to a wider audience and reach new fans. Additionally, the introduction of hybrid technology has allowed teams to utilize both internal combustion engines and electric cars, making the competition more diverse and challenging for the teams. The hybrid technology has also allowed for more sustainable and environmentally friendly racing, which is an important consideration for the championship's future. Overall, the championship has adapted by expanding its scope and incorporating new technology to keep up with the changing landscape of the sport.
