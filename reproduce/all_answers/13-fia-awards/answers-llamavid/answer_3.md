The integration of electric cars into the FIA World Rallycross Championship added an additional layer of excitement and competitiveness to the 2024 season. 

Prior to 2024, the championship was dominated by internal combustion engine cars. However, with the introduction of electric cars as part of the championship, it opened up a new dimension of competition. This meant that the races became more intense and competitive, with the top drivers having to be more skilled and adaptable to be competitive. The electric cars also provided more opportunities for overtaking and passing, as the cars had a higher top speed and acceleration.

In addition, the electric cars allowed for more opportunities for close battles between the top drivers, as they were able to challenge each other more frequently during the races. The championship became more dynamic and exciting as a result of the added competition from the electric cars.

So in summary, the integration of electric cars into the championship made the races more competitive and exciting, as the drivers had to adapt to the new challenges presented by the electric cars.
