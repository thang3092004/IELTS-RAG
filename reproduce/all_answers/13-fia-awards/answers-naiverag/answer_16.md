### Promoting Accessibility and Affordability

One of the key aspects of grassroots motorsports support from the FIA is the emphasis on making motorsport more accessible and affordable. As noted in the discussions surrounding Rwanda's initiative, traditional entry into motorsport often comes with significant financial barriers, making it challenging for aspiring racers from diverse backgrounds to participate. The FIA is addressing this by providing resources, such as training programs and frameworks that lower costs, thereby enabling more individuals to engage with the sport from an early age. By making motorsport training more affordable, the FIA ensures a broader base of participants, which is vital for the sport's longevity.
