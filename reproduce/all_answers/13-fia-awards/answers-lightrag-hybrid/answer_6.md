The FIA (Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile) has made significant strides in promoting diversity and inclusivity in motorsports, particularly highlighted during the 2024 season and its associated awards ceremony. This commitment is evident through various initiatives and the overall atmosphere cultivated during events such as the FIA Awards 2024 held in Kigali.

### Engagement and Representation

One of the key aspects of the FIA's approach in 2024 is the emphasis on engaging diverse groups within the motorsports community. In the awards ceremony, the audience comprised attendees from approximately 120 countries, reflecting a rich mix of cultures and backgrounds. This diversity was not merely in numbers; it was instrumental in creating a celebratory atmosphere where various achievements were recognized, emphasizing inclusivity in the motorsport environment. 

Additionally, the presenters, including notable figures in motorsports, actively engaged with the audience and highlighted achievements across different demographics. The visual representation at the awards ceremony showcased not just the typical motorsport personalities but also included women and individuals from various backgrounds, contributing to the broader representation within the sport.

### Initiatives and Recognition

The FIA has also initiated specific awards and recognitions aimed at celebrating diversity and fostering an inclusive culture. The 2024 FIA Awards included categories and presentations designed to honor achievements that contribute to a more equitable motorsport landscape. This includes acknowledging not only the champions like Lando Norris but also emerging talents and significant contributors from diverse backgrounds, thereby underscoring the FIA's dedication to fostering inclusivity.

Furthermore, the inclusion of specific sessions within the awards that addressed themes of diversity and community support underscores a commitment to making motorsports accessible. The presence of discussions around diversity during the event indicated an ongoing effort to raise awareness and advocate for inclusive practices in motorsports.

### Conclusion

In conclusion, the FIA’s approach to diversity and inclusivity during the 2024 season is evidenced by the engagement of a diverse audience, the recognition of various contributors to the motorsport community, and the promotion of a celebratory and inclusive atmosphere at the FIA Awards 2024. These facets highlight the FIA's ongoing commitment to driving change within the motorsport industry, paving the way for a more inclusive future.
