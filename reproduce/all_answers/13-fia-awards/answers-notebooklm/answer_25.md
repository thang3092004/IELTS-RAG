The FIA Awards ceremony in Kigali, Rwanda, incorporated cultural performances to enrich the event and showcase the host country's artistic heritage. The National Ballet of Rwanda opened the ceremony with a performance that highlighted the country's cultural traditions. Later in the evening, Rwandan artists Kenny Solheimano and Bukuru delivered a musical performance, further emphasizing the nation's artistic talents.

**Integrating these performances into the awards ceremony provides a platform to celebrate and share Rwandan culture with a global audience**. This approach not only enhances the entertainment value of the event but also fosters cultural understanding and appreciation. By showcasing these artists, the FIA Awards ceremony acknowledges the rich artistic heritage of Rwanda and its contribution to the global arts scene.
