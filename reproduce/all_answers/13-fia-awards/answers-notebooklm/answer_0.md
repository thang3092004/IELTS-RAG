The 2024 World Rallycross Championship featured a "Battle of Technologies" where internal combustion engine cars raced against fully electric vehicles. This presented a challenge for drivers like Johan Kristofferson, who chose to drive an internal combustion engine car and ultimately won the championship. He noted that competing against a different technology was a "huge challenge" but also "really fun".

The advancements in Formula 1 technology also played a significant role in the season.  Ferrari and Mercedes significantly improved their cars, making the competition more difficult for Red Bull and Max Verstappen. This led to a more unpredictable season with multiple teams vying for the top spot. The development of faster cars, like the McLaren, resulted in more intense and exciting races.
