The FIA Formula 2 Championship is a crucial stepping stone for aspiring Formula 1 drivers, as evidenced by the experience of **Gabriele Bortoleto, the 2024 F2 champion who secured an F1 seat with Sauber for 2025**. The championship is the final step in the FIA's single-seater racing ladder, providing a highly competitive environment that prepares drivers for the demands of F1. 

Bortoleto's journey exemplifies the importance of F2:

* He first won the FIA Formula 3 championship in 2023.
* This success propelled him to Formula 2, where he again dominated and won the 2024 title.
*  **This consistent success, demonstrating his skill and adaptability, earned him the coveted F1 drive**.

The intense competition in F2, highlighted by the extremely close title fight Bortoleto experienced, mirrors the challenges of F1. This experience helps young drivers hone their skills, racecraft, and mental fortitude in preparation for the top level of motorsport.
