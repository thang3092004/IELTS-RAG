Cultural performances, such as those by Kenny Solheimano and Bukuru, play a significant role in enriching the FIA Awards ceremony by providing entertainment and a unique cultural experience for attendees. These performances showcase the artistic heritage of the host country, offering a glimpse into its traditions, music, dance, and storytelling. By featuring local artists, the ceremony not only celebrates the achievements of the award recipients but also highlights the cultural diversity and richness of the host nation, fostering a sense of pride and cultural exchange among the international audience.
