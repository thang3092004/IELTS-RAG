Social media and fan engagement have significantly influenced the FIA's selection process for the 'Action of the Year' awards. With the rise of social media platforms, fans have a more direct and immediate way to express their opinions and preferences. This increased interaction allows the FIA to gauge public sentiment and popularity of certain actions or moments in motorsport. Fan engagement through voting and sharing on social media can amplify certain actions, making them more likely to be considered for the award. Additionally, social media provides a platform for fans to campaign for their favorite moments, potentially swaying the selection process. Overall, social media and fan engagement have made the selection process more democratic and reflective of public opinion.
