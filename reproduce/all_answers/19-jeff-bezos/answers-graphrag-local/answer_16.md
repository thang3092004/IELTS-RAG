### Developing a 'Crisp Document' for Meetings at Amazon

At Amazon, the preparation and utilization of a 'crisp document' is a structured process that takes place before meetings to facilitate effective discussions and decision-making. This procedure is anchored in the company's commitment to thoroughness and clarity, ensuring that all participants arrive informed and ready to engage.

#### 1. Creation of the Six-Page Memo

The foundational element of a 'crisp document' is the Six-Page Memo. This memo serves as a narrative-driven document that outlines the key points to be discussed and is structured to provoke thoughtful dialogue among team members. Participants are expected to read this memo prior to the meeting, allowing them to familiarize themselves with the core topics and perspectives that will be addressed.

#### 2. Study Hall Approach

During meetings, Amazon employs a 'study hall' approach, wherein team members spend an initial period—typically around 30 minutes—reading the memo in silence. This time is allocated for individuals to absorb the information, take notes, and prepare questions or insights based on their reading. This silent reading phase is crucial as it sets the stage for an informed discussion, minimizing misunderstandings and miscommunications.

#### 3. Engaged Discussion

Following the silent reading session, the meeting transitions into an interactive discussion. Participants share their insights, pose questions, and engage in a collaborative dialogue around the ideas presented in the memo. This phase emphasizes open communication, with the goal of refining ideas and arriving at actionable conclusions. The structured discussion format encourages everyone’s participation, ensuring diverse viewpoints are considered.

#### 4. Iteration and Feedback

After the initial discussion, there may be a need for iterations of the memo based on the feedback and insights gathered during the meeting. This refinement process ensures that the document remains relevant and accurately reflects the team’s collective understanding and decisions. The emphasis on clear communication and crisp documentation not only aids in the current meeting but also sets a standard for future discussions.

#### Conclusion

The process of developing and refining a 'crisp document' at Amazon illustrates the company’s dedication to clarity and efficiency in its meeting culture. By utilizing the Six-Page Memo, adopting a study hall format, and encouraging engagement, Amazon ensures that its discussions are productive and focused on achieving tangible outcomes. This approach exemplifies an innovative method of handling meetings that fosters accountability and enhances collaboration among team members.
