Jeff Bezos, the founder of Amazon, studied electrical engineering and computer science at Princeton University, not physics. However, his technical background and analytical skills have significantly informed his approach to business and innovation. Bezos is known for his data-driven decision-making, long-term thinking, and willingness to experiment and take calculated risks. His scientific mindset has contributed to Amazon's culture of innovation, where experimentation and learning from failure are encouraged. This approach has led to the development of groundbreaking services and technologies, such as Amazon Web Services (AWS) and the Kindle e-reader.
