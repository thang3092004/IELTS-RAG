In the context of Amazon's corporate culture, 'truth-telling' refers to the emphasis on transparency, honesty, and direct communication. Amazon encourages its employees to speak candidly and share their opinions openly, even if it means challenging the status quo or disagreeing with others. This approach is intended to foster an environment where ideas can be freely exchanged, leading to better decision-making and innovation. Truth-telling is aligned with Amazon's leadership principles, which prioritize customer obsession, long-term thinking, and a commitment to high standards.
