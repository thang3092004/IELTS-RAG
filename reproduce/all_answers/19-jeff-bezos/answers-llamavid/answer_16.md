Jeff Bezos emphasizes the importance of creating a crisp document for meetings at Amazon. A crisp document is a one-page document that summarizes the main points of a meeting, including the key decisions, goals, and action items. He describes the process of creating one as follows:

1. Start with a high-level summary of the meeting, including the purpose and key takeaways. 

2. Next, list the key decisions, goals, and action items in bullet point format. 

3. Include any relevant data or metrics that support the decisions and goals.

4. Provide a summary of the key arguments or reasoning behind the decisions and goals.

5. Include any relevant background information or context to put the decisions and goals in perspective.

6. Consider adding visuals to help explain the decisions and goals.

7. Review the crisp document with the team to ensure it is clear, concise, and easy to understand.

8. Revisit the crisp document after the meeting to ensure the key decisions, goals, and action items were properly communicated and implemented.

The crisp document helps ensure that the team is aligned and understands the main points of the meeting. It is a useful tool for managing meetings and communicating decisions and goals.
