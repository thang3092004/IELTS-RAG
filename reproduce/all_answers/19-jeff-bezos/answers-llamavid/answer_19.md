The 'overview effect' refers to the profound sense of weightlessness and awe that astronauts experience when looking back at Earth from space. This experience has had a significant impact on Bezos's perspective on space exploration. In his words, "When you go to space, you don't go to heaven when you die, you go to heaven when you're born. You see all this blackness, and you talk to your mom and it's just an epic nature to it." This feeling of weightlessness and awe from space has led Bezos to become obsessed with space exploration and building the necessary infrastructure to make it accessible to the next generation. He believes that moving humanity into space is the only way to avoid stasis and ensure that civilization can continue to grow and evolve. The overview effect has reinforced his mission-driven approach to building Blue Origin and taking humans to Mars.
