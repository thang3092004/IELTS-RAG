Bezos's 'Day One' philosophy is a customer-focused approach to decision making, which prioritizes the needs of the customer over all other factors. This philosophy is based on the idea that the customer is always right and the job of the company is to serve the customer. The 'Day One' mentality is driven by the belief that the customer is the most important stakeholder, and that the company must always put the customer first. It emphasizes that the customer should be the primary focus of all decisions, and that the company should make decisions based on what is in the best interest of the customer, rather than what is best for the company. In essence, the customer should be the North Star that guides all decision making.
