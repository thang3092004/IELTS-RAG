Bezos's philanthropic efforts, such as his commitment to homelessness, education, and environmental initiatives, can be seen as an extension of his business philosophy of customer obsession. He believes that the customer should be the focus and that by putting the customer first, the company will thrive. By addressing social issues like homelessness, Bezos is trying to improve the lives of customers and their families. He also focuses on education to improve customer skills and knowledge. And he is investing in space to allow customers to access the benefits of space exploration. By giving away a portion of his wealth and investing in Blue Origin, Bezos is trying to create a self-sustaining ecosystem that will allow future generations to access space, just as he did with Amazon. His commitment to philanthropy and space exploration shows that he is trying to build a legacy that will outlive him and benefit future generations.
