Bezos's early experiences in Montessori schools likely shaped his views on education in several ways. The Montessori method emphasized self-directed learning, creativity, and problem-solving skills. Bezos has spoken about how this approach helped him develop an early sense of independence and resourcefulness. He has also said that the "overview effect" of seeing Earth from space, which he experienced on his first space flight, made him appreciate the beauty and fragility of the planet. These early experiences likely contributed to his later interest in creating a "world's most customer-obsessed company" and his emphasis on building infrastructure that can be used by future generations.
