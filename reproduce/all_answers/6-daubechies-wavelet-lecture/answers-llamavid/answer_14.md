Daubechies highlights the potential for time-frequency analysis in the context of fetal health, particularly for diagnosing fetal heart problems. He mentions that time-frequency analysis can be used to detect any abnormalities in the fetal heartbeat signal, which could indicate problems with the heart. By analyzing the frequency spectrum of the heartbeat signal, time-frequency analysis can reveal potential issues like arrhythmias, conduction problems, or other heart problems that could affect the baby's health. This could be very useful for doctors trying to monitor fetal health during pregnancy and detect any potential issues before birth. The technique is already being used in clinical applications to help diagnose fetal heart problems and plan interventions if needed.
