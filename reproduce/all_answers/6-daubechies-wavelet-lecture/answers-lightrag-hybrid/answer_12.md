### Principles of Synchrosqueezing

Synchrosqueezing is a technique that refines the time-frequency analysis of signals by reallocating the energy concentrated in the time-frequency spectrum. This method adjusts the representation based on instantaneous frequency information, allowing for a clearer distinction between different oscillatory components in a signal. By maximizing the clarity of frequency peaks, synchrosqueezing can effectively isolate the fetal heart rate from the maternal ECG.
