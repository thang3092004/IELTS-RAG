## Time-Frequency Analysis in Fetal Health Monitoring

Ingrid Daubechies highlights the application of time-frequency analysis specifically for the analysis of fetal heart rate. This technique is significant for monitoring the health of the fetus during pregnancy.

### Understanding Fetal Heart Rate Analysis

Time-frequency analysis involves utilizing advanced mathematical methods to interpret cardiac signals sourced from electrocardiogram (ECG) data. This process aims to uncover patterns within the fetal heart rhythms that are crucial for assessing fetal well-being or potential distress. By analyzing these signals, healthcare providers can gain insights into the health status of the fetus, enabling timely interventions if any issues are detected.

### Implications for Fetal Health Monitoring

The ability to effectively analyze fetal heart rhythms through time-frequency analysis may enhance the understanding of fetal health. Such insights may help in making informed medical decisions, thus ensuring better outcomes for both the fetus and the mother. This innovative approach underscores the integration of mathematical techniques into medical practices, demonstrating the interdisciplinary nature of modern healthcare.
