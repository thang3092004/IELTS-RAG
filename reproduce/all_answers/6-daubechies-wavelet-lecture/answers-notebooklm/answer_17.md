Daubechies explains that **phase lines in the windowed Fourier transform converge to points where singularities occur in the signal**. She visualizes this phase information by graphing the phase factor of the complex windowed Fourier transform output and mapping it as:

*   White if the magnitude is between -π/2 and π/2
*   Black when it is in the other half. 

This creates a zebra pattern in the plot, and Daubechies states that **the phase lines converge to the singularity points**.
