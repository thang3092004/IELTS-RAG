Guangzhou is a city with a rich history of architectural styles that have influenced its development. The city was once an important port city in the 19th and 20th centuries, with many foreigners living and doing business there. This is evident in the colonial architecture that remains to this day. The Starbucks located in the city is a perfect example, with its colonial-style building that feels like it could be in Vietnam. The city's modern development is also evident in the skyscrapers that dot the landscape. The mix of old and new architecture gives the city a unique charm.
