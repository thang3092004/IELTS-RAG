The vloggers described Urumqi's Grand Bazaar as **beautiful but chaotic, more set up for tourism than a place for locals**. They noted the **"Middle East atmosphere"** created by the color of the bricks and the shops selling rocks and dried fruits.  However, they found the bazaar to be a **"bit chaotic"** and **"not much to see,"** though they acknowledged the beautiful architecture. They concluded it is **"really set up for tourism,"** with **"lots of good photo opportunities"** and **"lots and lots of souvenir choices."**
