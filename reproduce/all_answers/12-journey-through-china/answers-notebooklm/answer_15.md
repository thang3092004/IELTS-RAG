* The spa provided a wide array of amenities and activities, including hot springs, saunas, indoor swimming pools, game rooms, cinemas, massage services, and a vast buffet with diverse food options.

* The spa's buffet was a highlight, offering unlimited snacks, drinks, and meals throughout the day.  The vloggers were impressed by the quality and variety of food, noting the premium beef, fresh seafood, and abundance of options.

**Overall Experience:**

* The vloggers described the spa as a "lifestyle" that they could get used to. They found it to be incredibly relaxing, luxurious, and convenient.
* Their other accommodations served as comfortable bases for exploring different cities and regions but didn't offer the same level of pampering and indulgence as the spa.
