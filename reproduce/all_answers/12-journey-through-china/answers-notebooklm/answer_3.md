The vloggers described their experience in Chengdu as much easier than their time in Xinjiang. In Chengdu, they felt **the city was "fun and convenient" because they could easily get around using bicycles, just like they had in Shanghai.** They did not have to take the metro at all. In contrast, they said **traveling around Xinjiang was "a bit more difficult" due to the lack of public transportation.** The vloggers had to rely on private drivers to take them to different sites because they were far apart and inaccessible by public transport. The vloggers found that **many tourists take tours in Xinjiang for this reason.**

 Another difference was that **the vloggers were able to find more activities to do in Chengdu, such as going to spas, compared to their time in Xinjiang.** For example, the vloggers went to a hot spring lounge and spent 18 hours enjoying the many amenities, such as the buffet and massage services. They also remarked that Chengdu has many hot pot restaurants and places to shop.
