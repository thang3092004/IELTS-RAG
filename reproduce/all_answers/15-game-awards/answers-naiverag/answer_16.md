### Music as an Integral Element of Gaming

Music in video games has evolved beyond mere soundtracks; it has become a pivotal aspect of the gaming experience itself. The presence of celebrated performers at the Game Awards amplifies this connection. For instance, the orchestral performances, such as those conducted by notable musicians like Trent Reznor and Atticus Ross, solidify the notion that video game scores can stand alongside traditional film scores. These performances demonstrate a recognition that the music creates emotional depth, enhances storytelling, and immerses players in the gaming world.
