The success of 'League of Legends' and its adaptation, 'Arcane', reflects the growing influence of multimedia franchises in the gaming industry by demonstrating how a popular game can expand its universe and reach a broader audience through different media formats. 'League of Legends', as a game, has built a massive player base and community, and its adaptation into the animated series 'Arcane' has allowed it to tap into the entertainment industry, reaching viewers who may not be gamers. This cross-media expansion helps to strengthen brand loyalty, increase revenue streams, and enhance the cultural impact of the franchise. It also highlights a trend where gaming companies are increasingly investing in multimedia storytelling to create immersive and expansive worlds that engage audiences across various platforms.
