The announcement of "The Witcher 4" has indeed added clarity to the speculation surrounding the game's development and its protagonist, particularly in light of previous leaks and rumors. The commentary and visuals from various events, especially surrounding the reveal at The Game Awards, indicate a strong confirmation of earlier expectations.

### Confirmation of Development
Prior to the official announcement, there had been numerous leaks and hints circulating in the gaming community about the possibility of "The Witcher 4" being in development. Many fans speculated that the series would continue from the narrative established in "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt," especially regarding characters like Geralt of Rivia and Ciri. The confirmatory language used during the reveal, suggesting that “time for a new saga” and indicating Ciri would play a central role, aligns directly with these prior rumors. This not only supports the existence of "The Witcher 4" but verifies that it is indeed a continuation of the franchise, further emphasizing CD Projekt Red's commitment to building upon the rich narrative the previous games established.

### Protagonist Insights
One of the most significant confirmations from the announcement was the affirmation that Ciri will be the protagonist in "The Witcher 4." This detail effectively validates long-standing speculations that arose from leaks describing her as a central character, especially due to her notable development throughout "The Witcher 3." The development team acknowledged during the presentation that Ciri’s journey would be pivotal, thus solidifying her role amidst the backdrop of a new story arc that diverges from Geralt’s narrative.

### Conclusion
In summary, the announcement of "The Witcher 4" serves to confirm several previously discussed leaks and rumors about its development and the focus on Ciri as the protagonist. The gathering excitement surrounding this new installment, enhanced by lively reactions from fans and commentators, underscores the anticipation surrounding its release while building upon the established lore of the Witcher series. As CD Projekt Red begins to unveil more details in the lead-up to the game's launch, the community's interest remains high, with many eager to see how this new saga will unfold.
