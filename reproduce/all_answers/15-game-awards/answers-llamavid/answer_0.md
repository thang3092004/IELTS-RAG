According to Hideo Kojima, the role of the Game Awards has evolved over the past 10 years in several ways. First, it has grown to become a significant international event, with more nominees and winners from around the world. Second, it has become an important platform for developers to announce their new games and projects. For example, Kojima mentions that he will announce the new game he is working on, Death Stranding 2, at the Game Awards. Third, the Game Awards has evolved in terms of its influence, with the industry recognizing and rewarding the work of developers and players. Kojima notes that the Game Awards has helped shape the industry and bring together different ideas into a single cohesive work. Finally, Kojima sees the Game Awards as an opportunity to support the game direction, which is important for the future of the industry.
