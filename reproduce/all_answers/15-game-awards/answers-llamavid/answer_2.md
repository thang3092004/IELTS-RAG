Over the past decade, there have been a few recurring trends in the types of games that have been nominated for Game of the Year. Some of the most common types of games include:

1. Action-Adventure games. These have been represented by games like The Last of Us Part II, God of War, and Uncharted 4. They often feature strong narratives and immersive worlds. 

2. First-person shooters. Games like Call of Duty: Modern Warfare, Battlefield V, and Apex Legends have been frequently nominated for Game of the Year. These games tend to have fast-paced gameplay and focus on multiplayer.

3. Open-world RPGs. Games like The Witcher 3, Skyrim, and Fallout 76 have been popular choices for Game of the Year. They provide players with vast, expansive worlds to explore.

4. Horror games. Games like Resident Evil Village, Hellblade, and Outlast have been nominated for Game of the Year, often featuring dark, atmospheric settings and tense gameplay.

5. Simulation games. Games like Farming Simulator, The Sims 4, and Cities: Skylines have been recognized for their realistic gameplay and immersive worlds.

In summary, the most common types of games nominated for Game of the Year have been action-adventure, first-person shooters, open-world RPGs, horror games, and simulation games, though there have been some outliers over the years.
