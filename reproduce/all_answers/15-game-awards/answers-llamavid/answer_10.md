The acceptance speeches of developers like Nicolas Doucet ('Astrobot') at the Game Awards highlight the collaborative nature of game development. When accepting the award for Best Game Direction, Doucet thanked not just his own team, but also the other developers involved in the game. He acknowledged "every single person on the team coming and really bringing their own vision and being responsible for their own work." This shows the importance of teamwork and collaboration in game development, not just individual contributions. Doucet also emphasized the "idealism" of the developers involved, indicating they wanted to create a game that they would enjoy playing themselves. This shows the developers had a shared vision for the game and worked together to make it happen. So the acceptance speeches demonstrate the collaborative nature of game development and the importance of teamwork in creating a good game.
