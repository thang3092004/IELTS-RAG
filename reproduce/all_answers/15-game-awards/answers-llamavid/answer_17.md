The 2024 Game Awards were a significant event that defined major trends in the video game industry. Some of the major trends were:

1. The announcement of new games: The Game Awards showcased a variety of new games that were either released or will be released in the future, including the highly anticipated Elden Ring from FromSoft. This highlighted the importance of new game announcements as a draw for players and fans. 

2. The recognition of indie developers: The 2024 Game Awards recognized indie developers with various awards, highlighting the growing impact of indie games. This trend suggests that the industry is becoming more inclusive and diverse.

3. The focus on co-op games: Many of the major announcements and game of the year winners were co-op games, showing that cooperative multiplayer experiences are a major trend in gaming.

4. The return of beloved franchises: Many popular franchises were showcased at the Game Awards, including the return of Shovel Knight and the announcement of a new game in the Resident Evil series. This suggests that nostalgia for classic games is still a major trend.

5. The shift to mobile gaming: The Game Awards showcased mobile games, highlighting the growing importance of mobile gaming and its impact on the industry.

In summary, the major trends defined by the 2024 Game Awards were new game announcements, recognition of indie developers, a focus on co-op games, the return of beloved franchises, and the shift to mobile gaming. These trends demonstrate the changing landscape of the video game industry.
