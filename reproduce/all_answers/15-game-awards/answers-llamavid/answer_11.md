The trailers and announcements at this year's Game Awards show suggest several games are likely to generate significant discussion and anticipation among players. Some of the most exciting upcoming games include:

- Elden Ring from FromSoft, which promises to be a "co-op survival game" set in the Soulslike universe. This is the long-awaited sequel to the beloved Dark Souls series.

- Night Raid fromSoft, which is being billed as a "roguelike with a battle royal" and promises a "survival game" experience. It features a battle bus that drops players into the map to battle other players. 

- Wukong from Astrobot, which is being billed as the "game of the year" and promises an "immersive experience" set in an "epic fantasy world."

- The revival of the classic game Okami from Clover Studios, which promises a "masterpiece of a game" with "inspiration from a wide range of genres and titles."

- The revival of the classic game Shadow the Hedgehog from Sonic Team, which promises a new game set in the Sonic the Hedgehog universe.

All of these games show promising gameplay and feature innovations that are likely to generate excitement among fans of the respective franchises.
