The success of *League of Legends* and its Netflix adaptation *Arcane* illustrates the **expanding impact of multimedia franchises within the gaming industry**.

* The Game Awards 2024 recognized *League of Legends* as the **Best Esports Game**. This award highlights the game's sustained popularity and competitive scene, which translates to a large, dedicated fanbase. 
* *Arcane*, the animated series based on *League of Legends*, received the award for **Best Adaptation** at The Game Awards 2024. The show’s critical acclaim and popularity demonstrate the potential for successful transmedia storytelling, expanding upon the game's lore and characters to engage a broader audience.
* *Arcane's* success was so significant that one commentator believed it was a guaranteed win for the award, highlighting the show's impact.

These accolades underscore a trend where **popular games are no longer confined to a single medium**. By branching out into television, film, and other forms of media, gaming franchises can:

* Deepen audience engagement
* Attract new fans
* Generate additional revenue streams

The example of *League of Legends* and *Arcane* showcases the **potential for multimedia franchises to elevate the gaming industry's cultural relevance and financial success**.
